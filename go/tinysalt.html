TinySalt is a fresh and elegant blog theme for food blogger, cooking lovers and small food business. Idea for all food bloggers, whether you have a new blog or a blog that already has hundreds of recipes. Present your content in a beautiful way.

TinySalt provides rich blog styles and layouts. And it is easy to create and manage your recipes. The theme is compatible with popular plugins such as WooCommerce, MailChimp for WordPress, Contact Form 7, and more. With WordPress customizer, you can easily change colors, fonts, and more theme appearance with instant preview.

12 Pre Designed Demos

Select a demo and import it to your website with simple click. The layouts in different demos can be combined.

Friendly & Powerful Recipe Management

Powered by WP Recipe Maker, the most popular free recipe plugin with 30000+ active users. You can present your recipes step by step with photos, including all necessary recipe meta. Readers can review, rate, print and share recipes. And it uses schema.org/Recipe JSON-LD metadata optimised for Google Recipe search.

Instant Search Recipe Index

Display all recipes on the Recipe Index page, with instant Ajax search and filters. Readers can enter keywords to search, or filter recipes by categories/cuisines/courses.

Fully Compatible with Elementor

Since version 3.0, TinySalt is 100% compatible with Elementor – One of the most popular WordPress page builders. Elementor-version demo data are included in the theme.

WordPress 5 & Gutenberg Ready

TinySalt is ready for WordPress 5 and the new intuitive drag and drop core editor – Gutenberg, to make sure that you will have a smooth writing experience.

Promotion & Advertising

Monetize your food blog with TinySalt. You can insert advertisement (custom image with link or Google AdSense scripts) into different places across the website. Wanna promote your products or events? Just use our built-in home widgets: Call To Action and Featured Boxes.

Sell on Your Food Blog

TinySalt is compatible with WooCommerce. Build your own online shop and start selling now.

Regular Updates

Created and maintained by active Elite author. Update regularly of continuously improve our products. When an update is available, you can update the theme directly from your WordPress dashboard with simple clicks. No hassle to download and upload manually.

Full Feature List

  • Instant Ajax Search & Filter for Recipe Index page.
  • Readers can rate, print, share recipes.
  • Informative cooking steps with photos.
  • Google Rich Snippets, improve search ranking and SEO.
  • Engage with readers by displaying more related recipe posts after each post.
  • Flexible theme options to create different layouts and styles.
  • Easily customize the theme and instant previews with Customizer.
  • Fully responsive design, and retina ready. Sharp and beautiful on any device.
  • Featured recipes in Homepage Top Slider with 6 different layouts.
  • Multiple Posts Layouts to create diversity.
  • 8 Home Widgets to create your own unique homepage. Each home widgets contains many options for content, layout and appearance.
  • 3 site header layouts with color choices. Supports mega menu (displays latest posts with thumbnails in the drop down menu).
  • Light & Dark color schemes. Custom accent color.
  • Unlimited colors for most elements.

  • Standard pagination, Load More pagination, Infinite Scroll pagination.

How long does it take for LoftOcean support to reply to your support request?

We provide support for our customers Monday to Friday: 10 AM to 6 PM (UTC+10). Support requests will be processed in the order received.

  • Usually we reply to all requests within 24 hours.
  • However, sometimes we may receive a large number of requests, or some questions are complicated and require in-depth research to answer. In the above two cases, it may take more than 24 hours for us to respond.
  • If it is a weekend, our response time may take 48 hours or more.
