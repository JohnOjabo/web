Best Feminine WordPress themes

1. Divi

Divi is an incredibly customizable WordPress multipurpose theme that suits all imaginable needs and requirements. Divi will always have your back regardless of your website or business nature.

The creators designed Divi to be elegant and classy. They also designed its templates professionally to aesthetic and maritime excellence. Endless personalization with incredibly versatile plugins and tools makes Divi the theme of your personal or professional online dreams.

Divi’s unique, custom-page building interface allows you to create custom sections or modules with elements, plugins, and tool-powered functionalities. Then, drag and drop these modules or lay them out as desired.

At this point, you’d have to ask yourself: Is there anything Divi can’t do? (Answer: No!)

2. Gillion (Trending Item)

Gillion is a popular feminine WordPress theme oriented to magazines and publishing or writing. It uses WPBakery page builder and Unyson as a framework. You can get it with 15+ demos and a one-click install feature.

The developers made Gillion with a super potent admin panel that allows the customization of most aspects and elements. You get unlimited colors and multiple headers alongside many blog layouts.

Use more than 12 custom widgets and set up social media icons too. Moreover, Gillion is SEO improved and Bootstrap compatible. Also, it has excellent quality support from Google Analytics!

Gillion is ready to start a new project fast.

3. Jevelin

Jevelin is a clever feminine WordPress theme. Jevelin lets you customize every section of your web page with 40+ different elements and widgets. Flexible demo websites adapt to your every need in a few clicks.

Deploy portfolio showcases and engaging slideshows without breaking a sweat. A range of blog and portfolio layouts lets you arrange your content. You can also activate parallax effects, video backgrounds, and much more.

You can brand and style Jevelin effortlessly. Custom author pages and post styles allow your websites to express your unique voice. Extensive admin panel settings let you turn Jevelin websites inside out instantly.

Widgetized sidebars, footers, and more afford you the flexibility you need. Plus, integrated feeds bring your social media presence right into your website.

Check out Jevelin today and discover why it’s so powerful first-hand!

4. MagPlus

MagPlus is a crafty WordPress theme for anyone with a dream. It offers 40+ stylish demos with topic variations that feature feminine design. Customization is as easy as saying “yes” with responsive design, one-click import, and video tutorials.

Build, arrange and post with a live customizer and codeless drag & drop functions. Customize layout elements with 18+ headers, 4+ footers, 30+ shortcodes, and the Yellow Pencil editor. Magplus offers unlimited colors, sidebars, blog pages, and premium typography. The integration of WooCommerce allows you to set up shops with everything from wishlists to carts.

Furthermore, Magplus supports every posting format. External platforms for videos such as Vimeo, DailyMotion, and YouTube and sliders are also included.

Go for it, choose MagPlus! An SEO-optimized tool with documentation and updates provided for a quick start.

Bonus: What More Does Your Feminine Theme Need?

In addition to having a soft feel to its visual design, any feminine theme will also require the high-quality functionality expected from all WordPress themes created by reliable theme developers.
