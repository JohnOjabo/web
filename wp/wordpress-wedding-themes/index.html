22 Best WordPress Wedding Themes (2023)

  • April 9, 2023
  • Aigars
  • Theme Collections
  • 25 Comments

A comprehensive list of the best WordPress wedding themes in 2023. These have everything to make a unique wedding website that your friends and family will love.

We picked themes with different functionalities so that you can use them for photographers, event organizers, personal blogs, online stores, and beyond.

You will find themes with RSVPs, countdown timers, image and video galleries, personal story pages, etc.

They all share premium quality, functionality, pixel-perfect design, and unlimited customization.

Share emotions with your close friends and family about your special event via a lovely website.

Beautiful & Responsive WordPress Wedding Themes

1. Jevelin

Jevelin is a beautiful and elegant WordPress wedding theme. You can set up your website by installing one of the many built-in demo websites or template pages with a single-click installation process.

You can customize layouts, animations and elements through the visual drag-and-drop page builder Slider Revolution.

Couples love Jevelin for their wedding websites because it provides the flexibility of 10 different portfolio layouts, all easily deployable for beautiful image galleries and wedding photography in gloriously high resolution with smooth lightbox features, modern masonry and grid layouts, and far more beyond the hood.

Jevelin is the theme you want for your special day!

More info / Download Demo

2. Loveme

Wedding announcements, planners and shop websites are a piece of cake with Loveme. Let me tell you something: The design is so excellent you’ll want to use it as-is, saving time and energy.

How cool is that? (But this is just the beginning.)

Loveme has multiple demos for an instant start (with one click importer). It comes with regular updates, so you know your wedding-related website will run smoothly forever.

And you can modify anything you want with advanced (but user-friendly) theme options. Make everyone go “How lovely!” and spark their emotions with Loveme.

More info / Download Demo

3. Webify

You can gain massive value from a spectacular website as a wedding photographer. Instead of hiring someone to build a page for you, you can do it yourself with Webify.

Different samples are ready-made for you to use and have a page close to completion in just a few clicks.

Webify is perfect for someone who does not have time or is an utter beginner. Still, expert web developers will find Webify of great use, too.

The whole Webify structure is entirely modifiable via drag and drop page builder. You can also choose from different headers and footers, pre-defined blocks and elements. Establishing a magnificent wedding photography website will be as easy as pie with Webify.

More info / Download Demo

4. Gillion

Gillion is a wedding WordPress theme oriented to magazines, blogs and general writing. It helps you get the word out faster with many layouts, styles and responsive design. It is Unyson-based and compatible with Bootstrap.

This theme uses WPBakery page builder as the main editor with customization options. Colors are unlimited, social icons are integrated, and multiple headers are available.

You will find a website ready for translation and fully customizable with widgets. Get extensive documentation and premium support too!

More info / Download Demo

5. Kalium

With the high versatility of Kalium, you can effortlessly use it as a WordPress wedding theme. An exclusive sample is ready-made for you to use out of the box.

If you fancy the web design, feel free to employ the powerful and practical Kalium as is. Still, if you want to personalize it, do it with handy visual editing.

Outstanding full-screen banner, sticky navigation, invitation/RSVP form, back to top button, and engaging gallery are just some of the features you get with Kalium.

Also, Kalium’s demo is perfect for establishing a one-page wedding website. If you’d like to do things differently, do it with Kalium.

More info / Download Demo

6. Massive Dynamic

Massive Dynamic is an intuitive multipurpose WordPress wedding theme. The Live Website Builder puts the power of 70+ building blocks and 70+ demos to good use.

Massive Dynamic covers many specific, niche applications, including extraordinary wedding websites, among the numerous, elegant template designs.

Creative layout styles, such as full width, sidebars, or free-floating, present your content differently.

Outstanding portfolio features let you resize and reorder your best images in real-time as you build the masonry grid scrapbooks of your dreams. Get ready for the BIG day in style.

More info / Download Demo

7. Uncode

Uncode is a responsive WordPress wedding theme that provides simplicity and fast performance. It has dozens of homepages with a one-click demo import.

Uncode is a theme made for personalization with a creative touch. It suits artists and professionals that showcase their work. And you can do all commercial transactions through WooCommerce.

Moreover, the theme comes with parallax scrolling and powerful Slider Revolution.

Uncode, unlike others, features a media library packed with your favorite social media.

Play with footer variations, unlimited sidebars and customizable backgrounds. Documentation and tutorial videos are also available for any doubts. Start your website with Uncode confidently!

More info / Download Demo

8. Moments

Moments is a beautiful WordPress one-page wedding website theme. With an intuitive visual page builder and elegant theme options panel, it puts you in the driver’s seat of your wedding website’s look and feel.

Moments also includes unique, in-house developed sections specifically for weddings and couples, such as Couple Timelines, Event Timelines, Gift Section, Guestbook Timeline and RSVP Forms, among many others.

Bring your special moments online with Moments.

More info / Demo Demo

9. Sway

Sway never disappoints with its enormous collection of ready-to-use demos. If you are looking for a wedding planner WordPress theme, that’s exactly what Sway becomes with just a click.

You can now promote your services professionally online, even though you may be building your first website. No coding and design knowledge is necessary to succeed at creating a page with Sway.

Your website can have special effects, a portfolio, parallax scrolling, testimonials, blog, well, you get the gist – anything.

You can also collect emails with the newsletter subscription form. Not having a website is so old-school, so create one now with Sway.

More info / Download Demo

10. Gretna Green

Gretna Green is a lovely, modern and creative WordPress wedding theme. If you want to increase the hype for your weddings, do it in style with Gretna Green.

This page canvas is packed with goodies that will help you impress all your loved ones. Distinctly share your story and invite everyone to your D day.

Gretna Green has a nice collection of pre-built front and internal demos for you to put into play.

The creation will happen in a small breeze since you will never need to touch a single line of code.

All happens visually with WPBakery drag and drop page editor. And all the custom shortcodes and widgets with Gretna Green will speed things up even more.

More info / Download Demo

11. Jack & Rose

Jack & Rose is a classy WordPress wedding website theme.

Incorporating an easy-to-use premium drag-and-drop page builder, Jack & Rose makes short work of putting together impressive wedding websites.

Jack & Rose theme comes with beautifully pre-designed demo templates and pages you can import with a single click.

This theme has features like the guestbook, the online RSVP, fancy smooth parallax effects and Google Maps integration. Nobody does a wedding like Jack & Rose!

More info / Download Demo

12. Fleur

Creating a wedding website becomes a piece of cake once you put Fleur to use. This WordPress wedding theme was put together exclusively for celebrations and nuptials.

Fleur includes 13 demos which all are mind-blowing and easy to modify.

In many cases, the out-of-the-box look is the one that you would want to stick to. But you can enhance the design at free will.

Take everything wedding-related to an entirely new degree with Fleur.

More info / Download Demo

13. Bridge

Create your wedding event ten times more special with a spectacular website that you can also use as an RSVP. You can create a wedding website almost instantly with Bridge.

Import the tool with one click, and you can already start tailoring it to your needs. Your wedding page is ready to go live, sharing your story, images, and other useful information about the big event after a few tweaks.

You don’t need to code to craft the striking page you want to rock the online world.

Thanks to the drag and drop technique, anyone can utilize Bridge to its full potential and start spreading the word.

More info / Download Demo

14. Solene

Solene is a lovely WordPress wedding theme for all the photographers out there. If you want to expand your reach, you can now make it happen with the all-around and easy-to-use Solene.

There are many wowing samples to choose from, all fully flexible and adaptive.

Different portfolio layouts, gallery proofing, online store, blog, call-to-actions, 4 headers, and video backgrounds are some of the features of Solene.

You also get extensive documentation and access to Solene’s professional support team if you are a beginner.

More info / Download Demo

15. WedCo

WedCo is a full-blown WordPress wedding theme for nuptials, photography, invitation, catering and event planner, to name a few.

There are 5 demos and 50+ ready-made elements for you to work with as is or style them further. Indeed, everything is fully customizable, so everyone easily gets the most out of WedCo.

Slider Revolution, WPBakery page builder, MailChimp, Google Fonts and SEO optimization; it’s all part of the package.

However, this is still just a tiny segment of all the material that WedCo has in store for you. See the live demo and enjoy the power of WedCo first-hand.

More info / Download Demo

16. The Aisle

This multi-concept tool is ideal for wedding planners, couples, invitations, shops, and florist businesses. One web design for multiple different projects out of the box.

And when you introduce your personal touch, you can easily expand the possibilities of The Aisle.

The Aisle also sports RSVP form, stunning parallax effect, intuitive admin, one-click sample import and a team of experts who are always down to help you.

More info / Download Demo

17. Be Theme

Be Theme is a gorgeous mobile-friendly multipurpose WordPress theme. You can use it to build any website, including a wedding one.

To that end, Be Theme combines the power of the amazing Muffin Builder with WPBakery. Both high-end visual page builders come with 100s of elements, widgets and features.

You can use them to build your websites from scratch or fine-tune the 600+ completely designed, pre-built websites packed with Be Theme.

More info / Download Demo

18. Simple & Elegant

Simple & Elegant is a high-quality WordPress theme for creative businesses emphasizing photography. It has a one-click install demo and a child theme.

Simple & Elegant uses “simplicity through images” as its motto. Galleries, portfolios and blogs are the main relevant features, Lightbox, MailChimp and Parallax.

For page building and galleries display, you have WPBakery and Slider Revolution. Moreover, you can use either a boxed or wide/stretched layout.

Thanks to the off-canvas menu, it makes its mobile experience even better and supports mega menu.

You can use WooCommerce and pricing table to handle purchase matters.

Simple & Elegant makes things  – yes! – simpler.

More info / Download Demo

19. Newspaper

Newspaper is the perfect choice to share happiness with the rest of the world: reveal love stories, latest wedding trends, gossip, vendor offers, or honeymoon locations.

Everything is so easy with Newspaper 12! (Start with the ready-to-use wedding demo.)

Newspaper also has a bold page architecture and special features like mega menus, video playlists, post and page variations, headers, footers, etc.

Moreover, this elegant and modern wedding website template comes with a powerful and intuitive Theme panel to easily change the look and feel of your website.

Newspaper has an intelligent ad system to prepare your website AdSense in a few seconds.

You have endless possibilities to tell your stories to the world.

More info / Download Demo

20. Lavelo

Lavelo is a WordPress wedding theme that provides a quick solution for your fresh new project. It gives you access to 4 attractive homepages and two pre-designed header styles.

Everything you see Lavelo sports is custom made, from RSVP and photo gallery to bridesmaids, groomsmen, and others.

With Lavelo, you can build an extraordinary wedding website template to grab your audience’s attention.

By employing the drag and drop page builder, you can easily adjust and tailor Lavelo to your desires.

Other goodies of this theme are a sticky menu, Contact Form 7, animations, and in-depth documentation. Yes, Lavelo is also entirely responsive.

More info / Download Demo

21. Alis

Couples and event organizers looking to build a wedding website, consider using the versatile Alis. It is just a matter of you taking action to define how fast you can have a website up and running.

Alis has it all ready-made; mix and match the available and you are ready to get things rolling.

Alis provides multiple niche demos, premium stock images, dedicated icons, a drag-and-drop builder, and a live WordPress customizer.

You can quickly modify the look of Alis according to your needs and distinguish yourself from the masses.

More info / Download Demo

22. Wedding Couple

Regarding your wedding website, you need a theme to capture the essence of this life-changing moment.

Wedding Couple aims to inform your guests and impress them with a gorgeous design. It also has one-and multi-page layout option.

Your website will have many niche-specific functions, such as the ability to include a Wedding List. This allows the happy couple to add products to their wedding registry.

You can mark the locations on a built-in Google map with photos, titles, coordinates, and special descriptions.

This theme also facilitates the creation of RSVP forms.

Save time and build a fully functional website in a matter of moments.

More info / Download Demo
