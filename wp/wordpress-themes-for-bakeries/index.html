18 Best WordPress Themes for Bakeries 2023

  • June 26, 2023
  • Aigars
  • Theme Collections
  • 15 Comments

We hand-picked the best responsive WordPress themes for bakeries after thoroughly testing 50+.

When picking your favorite, it’s more about the design and style than anything else.

Hey (!), we ensured all are top-notch, reliable, and include many page layouts and features.

But that’s where the fun begins.

You can use live editing to perform customization tweaks in a few clicks. (You’ll never need to touch code.)

Building a powerful bakery website is way easier than you think.

Get busy baking goods with WordPress!

Best WordPress Themes for Bakeries

1. Divi Home Bakery

Divi is a flexible WordPress theme for bakeries with over 200 pre-made starter templates that you can use to build your website. It comes with a drag and drop page builder, allowing you to build websites visually. It can be used for local bakeries and eCommerce stores thanks to its WooCommerce support.

Preview Divi

2. Astra

Astra is the most popular WordPress theme ever released with over 1.6M active users. It works with Elementor, Beaver Builder and even Gutenberg to create a unique website for your bakery. This theme has a free version but, unfortunately, none of the bakery related started templates doesn’t come with the free version. Astra is still one of the most accessible website builders out there.

Preview Astra

3. Elementor Bakery Website Builder

Elementor is a drag and drop website builder with over 5M active users making it the most popular plugin for WordPress. It has evolved into a full website builder tooling that doesn’t require choosing a separate theme. You can choose their cloud hosting package with hosting, theme and page builder to get your website up and running in a few hours. Elementor Cloud is the fastest way to build a bakery website even if it is your first experience working with WordPress or any CMS.

Preview Elementor

4. Jevelin

Jevelin is a super versatile WordPress theme for bakeries and other food businesses. With its multiple demos and other predesigned elements and components, you can easily set up the best possible website for your business.

You can do a ton for your company and truly take it to an entirely new level with a website.

Of course, Jevelin is mobile-friendly and is optimized for the best performance. Jevelin creates a visually appealing online presence that will impress your visitors.

Realize all your ideas and plans with the powerful and practical Jevelin.

More info / Download Demo

5. Soledad

Soledad is an ideal choice for your bakery and recipes website. It offers many revolutionary features which aid in simplifying the site creation process.

Its WooCommerce plugin integration allows users to craft their professional e-shops.

Soledad’s search engine optimization is sure to get your name out there. (Extra online exposure!)

While most themes offer a bland color scheme, Soledad gives you a rainbow. Thanks to the WordPress Live Customizer, users access a limitless array of colors and configurations. (Preview changes in real-time.)

You can also implement Google Adsense in multiple locations of the pages.

Regardless of your settings, the results will always look incredible with Soledad.

More info / Download Demo

6. Webify

Share your baked goods online and reach folks from all over to visit your bakery with Webify. This WordPress theme for bakeries is packed with functional features and layouts for a quick start of a refreshing website.

Once you access this powerful tool, you can import the desired demo with a single click. From then on, all Webify requires is some dragging and dropping to make adjustments and improvements, which is pretty much it. (No coding, indeed.)

You will be amazed by how simple crafting and establishing a page for a bakery is with Webify. You skip the hard work, enjoying fine-tuning and branding the design only.

That’s that, take action now and make everyone’s mouth water.

More info / Download Demo

7. The Voux

The Voux has a stylish gallery option to upload pictures of your venues and tasty products. Every picture looks amazing in a full-screen format.

The Voux also features a user-friendly header with a powerful mega menu. You can implement categories, sub-categories, and tags to organize content neatly.

The installation and customization process takes you barely anytime, thanks to WPBakery page builder.

Feel free to access The Voux’s live preview, see it in full effect and go from there.

More info / Download Demo

8. Sway

Sway is a WordPress theme for many businesses, including bakeries and pastry shops. You can start working on your online presence with the dedicated demo and save yourself time.

Building a pro-level website happens without coding work, thanks to WPBakery drag-n-drop page builder. You can customize the look however you want until it matches your needs to a tee.

Sway also comes with regular updates (free for life!), video tutorials, and expert (but friendly!) support. If you’re ready to expand your bakery to a broader audience, build a site with Sway quickly (today!).

More info / Download Demo

9. Cake Art

Cake Art is a modern and responsive WordPress bakery theme. It is a beautiful tool with strong attention to detail.

Cake Art empowers webmasters of any skill level or background to effortlessly create the website of their dreams.

Whether you are a cook, a chef, a baker, or business owner looking to market your products, then Cake Art is the right choice for you.

Here you can showcase your craftsmanship through immersive high-resolution media presentation. This will likely have your visitors’ mouths watering and increase your business exposure.

Complete WooCommerce integration makes opening up a new revenue stream for your physical bakery or cake shop easy. Or, you can shop online and start baking your way to a sweeter world!

More info / Download Demo

10. Avada

Avada is a clean and attractive WordPress multipurpose theme for restaurants, cafes, diners, caterers and other food industry-related websites.

This theme bursts with tools and features to salivate your potential customers over your baked treats.

Build your online presence with gorgeous gallery layouts, unique transitions and animations, elegant pricing guides and gorgeous parallax effects.

Sort your content in any manner you please, from featured carousels that display your best products and proudest creations to the latest news and blog posts, searchable product galleries, and more.

Fulfill your dreams and fill some bellies with Avada!

More info / Download Demo

11. Craft

Craft is an exclusive WordPress theme that you can use for your coffee shop/bakery location. With Craft, you can create a pleasant online atmosphere that will wow all your visitors.

With its many predefined demos, you will quickly find the one that tickles your fancy. (Without the programming work!) from then on, you can either use it or improve it further.

Custom mega menu, header and footer builder, food menu, food/bakery delivery service with WooCommerce, you name it, Craft rocks it. A full-blown online booking solution is also available for your convenience.

More info / Download Demo

12. Crems

Crems is a modern and impactful bakery WordPress theme that calls for an epic outcome. With its dark design, everyone will easily immerse in your delicious specialties.

But do not forget to ensure that all your food photography is of the highest quality, as it will help with sales.

Integrating the Elementor page builder lets you quickly improve Crems to your needs and regulations. With full WPML support, you can also localize Crems without hassle.

Moreover, a few other neat features are the parallax effect, scrolling animations, Slider Revolution, and image zoom.

More info / Download Demo

13. Artemis

Artemis is one of the bestselling WordPress themes that suits most online and established businesses.

Artemis bakery, one of its niche demos, has a joyful yet elegant concept that makes your business stand out.

Colors and backgrounds are easy to change and make unique in style. You can create a food blog to get jumpstart community space conversations.

Lastly, you can ask the customer support team to help with your problems. Make your bakery business become the #1 of its type!

More info / Download Demo

14. Panadería

Panadería is very tasty and appealing WordPress theme for bakeries. There is a whopping collection of 11 samples and an additional demo coming soon to your advantage.

You can expect a first-class website set and ready to rock and roll pretty damn quick with Panadería. No need to do the work from scratch anymore.

Bakeries and pastry stores will adore the amazingness of Panadería. With the one-click installation process, you can pick the right demo and tailor it to your branding regulations.

You can also use Panadería to begin blogging and sell products straight from your freshly baked website. Use Panadería’s goodies in store for you and make a difference.

More info / Download Demo

15. Ogami

After all, your website is one of your best marketing hubs, offering everyone to learn more about you and even make purchases. With Ogami, you can now make a difference with the snap of a finger.

More info / Download Demo

16. Sweet Jane

Sweet Jane is originally a cake shop WordPress theme but it fits bakeries perfectly. You even get several ready-made demos, which are all stunning and tasty. They are all minimal and clean, perfect for showcasing your delicious creations.

You will create a neat online space for all the goodies you make with Sweet Jane. Let your potential customers drool over your images, dying to visit you and try the treats you offer.

You can easily modify Sweet Jane to follow your needs and regulations with the drag and drop page building.

The kit will also find many elements, internal page layouts, smooth transitions, a back-to-top button, and cool animations. Make Sweet Jane yours and shine online.

More info / Download Demo

17. Bellaria

Your tasty treats deserve a mouthwatering web design to display them all. Bellaria can help you achieve just that. It is a simple-to-use tool with many features that will get you live online quickly.

Bellaria boasts creativity and uniqueness with its distinctive touch, original shapes, and patterns.

There are 8 demos and a broad selection of other pages, elements, and styles you can mix and match with your creative touch.

The drag and drop page builder allows you to go as artistic as you want without touching a single string of code. Make your perfect bakery site quickly.

More info / Download Demo

18. Cakecious

As its name implies, Cakecious is a WordPress theme for bakeries and cake shops with a splendid design. It treats you to 5 neat and modern demos, and you can expect new variations to drop with future theme updates.

Other templates for about us, portfolios, blogs, services, and contact pages are available.

Cakecious has all you need to kick-start your bakery page. Different header styles, drag, and drop site editor, pixel-perfect coding, Contact Form 7, and WPML compatibility are additional functionalities Cakecious has ready and set for you.

What’s cool about Cakecious is that you can test it for 30 days straight and receive a refund if it does not suit you.
