Ultimate WordPress Themes For Musicians 2023

1. Lucille – WordPress Musician Theme

Lucille is a superb music WordPress theme with four pre-launched and configurable demos. (Dark & light layouts available.)

Media and posts publication is easy to manage, thanks to flexible templates. In addition, Lucille has a specific customizable design for video, photo, album, and event posting.

You can modify Lucille as a whole without coding by using WPBakery.

The translation is also easy with the WPML plugin. Widgets built in include SoundCloud, Discography and Music Band, and more.

You can add blogs and forums and take your online presence to the next level.

Enjoy the immersion in the world of the music industry with Lucille!

2. Vice – Underground Music WordPress Theme for Elementor

Vice is a WordPress theme dedicated to musicians and DJs. And the first theme of the market with site-wide video background and a site-wide interactive and customizable 3D background.

The Vice theme mainly focuses on the underground music scene, with an elegant and futuristic design.

This theme contains 8 custom widgets for Elementor to display carousels, sliders, and grids.

Building a music website with Vice is not only easy, but it’s also fun! Endless possibilities, cutting-edge technologies, and blazing-fast performance for the most intriguing music WordPress theme out there.

The new Vice is your theme if you are looking for something unique.
