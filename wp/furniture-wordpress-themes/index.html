15 Best Furniture WordPress Themes 2023

Welcome to our review of the best furniture WordPress themes for online stores and businesses.

We considered the industry standards during the testing phase, guaranteeing you only get the most reliable tools.

Unfortunately, we needed to exclude 15 themes.

Still, here are 15 that offer everything you need!

At this point, it’s not about one being better than the other – although the top 3 exceeded our expectations – it’s about picking the one that resonates with your company best.

Building a stand-out website happens quicker than you think with a suitable theme. (No technical skills necessary.)

Add your creative touch and furnish your furniture website like a pro. Yo!

Best WordPress Themes for Furniture eCommerce Stores

1. Webify

For everyone in the furniture space, you better push your services and sell items online with Webify. This magnificent furniture WordPress theme offers you to start your thing immediately.

You can be live in minutes if you keep the default settings of Webify and only enrich it with your content and details.

Of course, Webify is an entirely modifiable web design you can tailor to your needs and requirements to a T.

Webify also offers you to choose between different internal page layouts, multiple headers, and footers and a massive library of blocks and modules. You only truly need one theme to bring into being a high-class website – Webify!

2. Divi

If you want to create a furniture store, you are in for a treat with Divi. This multipurpose furniture WordPress theme is fully equipped with all the right elements and tools to sort out your ideas and plans.

You will have your own furniture website up and running in little to no time with the fantastic drag-n-drop page builder and ready-to-use material.

Make sure you utilize Divi to its fullest and see the magic happen. You will have an enticing and attention-grabbing page ready to quickly get your word out there.

Besides, Divi also comes with quick and reliable support and numerous other helpful contents to assist you on your successful website launch.

More info / Download Demo

3. Jevelin

Jevelin is a lightweight and responsive furniture WordPress theme. It is a resourceful framework for building awesome modern websites fast.

Jevelin wastes no time with sophisticated demo websites for a range of applications. Furthermore, you can pick your template and customize it at will with tons of widgets and elements.

Expand your website with powerful portfolios, blogs, store section and more. Users across all industries and applications find creative solutions in Jevelin.

The beautiful parallax visual effects keep your audience engaged and spruce up your content. Awesome video backgrounds and sliders provide an immersive navigational experience that WOWs everyone.

Give Jevelin a go today, and see for yourself!

More info / Download Demo

4. Addison

Addison is a clean furniture WordPress theme with 5 exclusive homepage designs and many internal pages.

You can craft a fully functional website for your furniture-related business without looking for any 3rd-party extensions with Addison. (It’s all carefully packed in its bundle of goodies.)

Plus, you can freely modify and adjust all of Addison’s sample material and incorporate your personal touch to it. The site canvas is also compatible with WooCommerce, comes with a cost calculator, different headers, sticky navigation, and blog and portfolio layouts.

Addison also includes in-depth documentation and customer support. You have everything ready-to-go with Addison. Yeah, GO!

More info / Download Demo

5. KALLYAS

KALLYAS is an impressively effective and responsive WordPress furniture theme.

It is also a potent website building platform with an in-house developed, plugin-free visual builder with 150+ customizable elements.

Moreover, it’s amazingly resourceful, with attractive parallax scrolling effects, gorgeous one-page and multi-page presentations, each with detailed configurations you can fiddle with to your hearts content.

Premium plugins like Slider Revolution add to the engaging atmosphere of your website, while convenient widgets and stunning hero scenes light up your pages and content instantly. MailChimp subscription newsletter integration is ready to start collecting new leads to take your business to the next level.

You can make the website you want every single time with KALLYAS.

More info / Download Demo

6. Bridge

Bridge comes with 3 super exclusive and impressive demos for your furniture projects. Each sample is unique in its own way, making Bridge one of the finest furniture WordPress themes available on the market.

Some of Bridge’s very many features and traits are parallax effect, testimonials slider, animated skillbars, back to top button and social media icons.

But this is just a small fracture of the entire bundle of goods and qualities you can get your hands on after you decide to go with Bridge.

Make your furniture website one-of-a-kind with Bridge and stand the test of time.

More info / Download Demo

7. Ekko

While you can start any website you want with Ekko, you can launch your online furniture shop with the included sample.

You will find nothing missing once you unpack the Ekko kit. From technicalities to remarkable front and internal layouts, Ekko has it all.

And you can also modify the look entirely or stick to the out-of-the-box version.

Ekko is responsive, cross-browser compatible and retina screen friendly, too. In addition to that, you also do not need to worry about fast loading speed and search engine optimization, Ekko takes care of them both.

Enjoy the powerful theme options and construct a modern furniture shop with Ekko today.

More info / Download Demo

8. Halena

Halena is a clean and minimal responsive WordPress multipurpose eCommerce website theme. This theme is a fast and simple platform for quickly creating functional, modern stores.

Setting up a shop is easy with Halena, regardless of your market or niche.

Halena’s furniture demo is a minimal and pristine framework to showcase your products.

Furniture stores, designers and manufacturers love working with Halena. Furthermore, an intuitive visual page builder makes short work of most design tasks.

Craft your website in minutes without writing a line of code.

More info / Download Demo

9. Rey

Rey is an innovative and all-around WordPress theme that you can employ for all sorts of online stores. And if you would like to sell furniture, you can do that, too.

You will find several flexible samples that you can take to your total advantage out of the box. On top of that, you can also benefit from Elementor, a page builder that does the coding work visually.

Tons of UI elements and inner pages, header and footer builder, mega menu, pop-ups, product quick view and modularity are all the other unique features that Rey treats you to.

Last but not least, with creative web design, Rey guarantees a shopping experience like none other.

More info / Download Demo

10. Auros

Auros is a dedicated furniture WordPress theme powered by Elementor and WooCommerce.

If you have the content ready, you will be done building your new eCommerce website in a small breeze. And, of course, you do not need to be a coder to make things happen.

In the bundle, you will discover a fantastic collection of 24 homepage layouts that will get you going quickly.

Moreover, Auros is mobile-ready, cross-browser compatible and retina screen-friendly. It also comes with several shop and blog page styles, so you really do not need to build anything from scratch.

More info / Download Demo

11. Konte

If the minimalist look is your cup of tea, Konte is the WordPress theme you will enjoy using. This multipurpose site canvas quickly acclimatizes to an assortment of different purposes. Yes, you can use Konte as a furniture WordPress theme, too.

There are 14 mesmerizing demos, 7 product and 11 shop pages, and 12 headers.

Additionally, Konte contains WPBakery page builder, Slider Revolution, WooCommerce, unlimited colors, built-in products filter and live search with suggestions.

There is all you need to complete creating your online furniture store swiftly. Konte also follows all the latest trends and regulations to secure you with a future-proof online presence.

More info / Download Demo

12. Konsept

Konsept is a stunning and striking furniture WordPress theme with many pre-built formats. The design is very appealing to the eye and fully configurable without the need to touch code.

Konsept is for beginners and professionals, offering you to create a top-class eCommerce furniture website.

All the necessary online store pages and elements, blog, portfolio, many shortcodes and sticky header are just a fraction of all the Konsept features. Documentation and professional support are also available, so you know site execution will be a breeze.

More info / Download Demo

13. Drile

Drile is an up-to-the-minute WordPress theme for furniture stores. Whether you would like to create a brand website or a general online furniture shop, it’s all possible with Drile. You get to work with any of the 10 available demos, all very beautiful and engaging.

Moreover, Drile leans more toward the minimalist style, ensuring a distraction-free product and content distribution.

Other features include Slider Revolution, WPBakery, different search styles, cart drop-down, video pop-up and RTL support. You can showcase your location with Google Maps integration and start a blog.

More info / Download Demo

14. Toro

Toro easily adapts to various businesses and projects. No doubt, if you are launching a furniture website, you can get things moving forward with it easily.

To quick-start your thing, 19 home and 51 supplementary page layouts will do the trick. Mix and match the available with your material and you will develop the winning solution quickly.

Furthermore, you can perform tweaks and improvements without any prior experience with WPBakery.

There is a lot of uniqueness and originality when browsing Toro’s live preview page.

You can import the desired demo content with a simple click and enhance it with your creative touches and items you want to push.

More info / Download Demo

15. Sheon

Sheon is a multipurpose WordPress theme with a wonderful collection of 16 demos. You can use it for all sorts of online stores including furniture.

Anyone can craft jaw-dropping pages even if without any prior experience using the drag and drop technology. (Sheon is super newbie-friendly.)

Some of the core features of Sheon are Slider Revolution, Contact Form 7 support, PSD files, outstanding support, portfolio layouts, and browser and device compatibility.

Drop-down shopping cart, product grid or list function, newsletter subscription widget, infinite product load and a collection of shortcodes are some of the extras you can use to your advantage.
