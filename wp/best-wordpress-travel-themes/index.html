      Of course you can go expensive routes and host your website with WP Engine, Pagely, Kinsta, Flywheel or other and your website will load extremely fast no matter which theme you will choose.       It is not possible to test themes on your own server before you buy them but you can use tools such as Pingdom and Google PageSpeed insights to test theme demo pages to see how well they perform.
