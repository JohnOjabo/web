16 Best Jewelry WordPress Themes For eCommerce 2023

  • January 9, 2023
  • Aigars
  • Theme Collections
  • 6 Comments

These jewelry WordPress themes are what you need to showcase your jewelry business and sell your products online.

Most customers will refuse to do business with a sub-standard website.

To inspire trust and encourage sales, you must ensure that your online presence is perfect. Typically, achieving such a level of quality would cost a lot of money. (Hiring a good web development company does not come cheap.)

BUT! That’s not the case for you.

These themes are not just visual upgrades, as they contain many practical features. They can facilitate online transactions, adapt your page to multiple devices, and enhance your website’s layout.

Jewelry shines even brighter with these WP themes!

Best WordPress Themes for Jewelry eCommerce Store

1. Divi

Suppose you are looking for a jewelry WordPress theme with boundless potential. Divi is a multilingual theme capable of displaying your content in any language.

Currently, it has been translated into 30+ languages, and it also has Right to Left writing support.

Every user can choose between creating a unique layout or installing one of the pre-made variations. You can make further changes, thanks to the fantastic Divi builder.

More info / Download Demo

2. WoodMart

WoodMart misses nothing in its massive bundle of page layouts, features, and plugins. And if you need a versatile jewelry WordPress theme, WoodMart is exactly what you’re searching for.

The amazingness of the theme surprised us when reviewing the goodies. The deeper into the theme we went, the more speechless we became.

Yes, there’s so much available! You can easily manage your entire online store with this one theme—no need for any 3rd-party tools and other special features.

Still, WoodMart is compatible with everything, so you can expand it however you want – no limits.

More info / Download Demo

3. Aurum

Aurum is a simplistic jewelry WordPress theme that facilitates the creation of an impressive eCommerce website.

There are several website variations, and you can also design your gorgeous skin via the Skin Builder. You can choose between 7 demos.

The excellent support staff can resolve any issue. In addition, the theme documentation source can walk you through the installation and customization process.

Aurum is compatible with the most popular translation and multilingual plugins, making it easy to translate your website.

More info / Download Demo

4. Massive Dynamic

Massive Dynamic is a well-developed and mobile-friendly platform for designing and creating beautiful jewelry eCommerce websites.

Deploying the potent Live Website Builder, Massive Dynamic puts the power of dozens of elements and demo websites at your disposal.

That makes Massive Dynamic a solid choice for a jewelry website, from mass-marketed jewelry bought and sold in bulk through SEO-enhanced product catalogs and portfolios to the most exquisite, handcrafted works of art.

More info / Download Demo

5. KALLYAS

KALLYAS is qualified to service the needs of jewelry shops, online jewelry retailers, artisanal jewelers and all related crafts and eCommerce endeavors.

The theme has convenient online shop page templates powered by the WooCommerce eCommerce plugin.

This theme comes with a visual frontend drag and drop page builder and dozens of example websites and pages you use as a base for your website.

More info / Download Demo

6. The Hanger

If we were to talk about what The Hanger can and cannot do, we would rather focus on the latter since there is barely anything this fantastic jewelry WordPress theme cannot.

It is a WooCommerce powered tool you can sell whatever you want.

The Hanger has an impressive and easy-to-use checkout process to collect new sales faster.

Besides, you can benefit from a vast assortment of features to take advantage of and make your online jewelry store recognizable from miles away.

Use stuff like coupons, one-click refunds, geo-location, tax options and shipping rates.

More info / Download Demo

7. Ekko

If you have the offline stuff sorted out, the time has come to take care of the online space. With Ekko, a jewelry WordPress theme, you can make things happen — like, right now.

Once you investigate the ready-to-use demo in great detail, you will notice one thing — nothing is missing.

A full-screen slider with a parallax effect is the first thing all your visitors will see. Spark their interest with professional imagery and encourage them to take action.

WPBakery page builder and WooCommerce plugin power Ekko. These two tools will help you manage and maintain your online jewelry shop effortlessly.

More info / Download Demo

8. The Luxury

The Luxury is a modern, streamlined theme for WordPress. It can showcase products such as bags, watches, cars, jewelry, and even yachts.

Every single item and offer will be listed using the WooCommerce plugin.

Site administrators can design unique product slides, showcasing promotions and offers via the Master Slider. This premium plugin would otherwise cost $28, yet Master Slider has included for free.

More info / Download Demo

9. Bijoux

Whether you are making jewelry or would like to resell it, Bijoux is a WordPress theme for building a jaw-dropping online store.

But you can also use Bijoux for something else with a few tweaks.

Moreover, Bijoux features the Elementor page builder so that even a beginner can create a top-notch outcome. Other amenities contain Slider Revolution, WPML integration, RTL support and one-click import.

There are tons of video tutorials and articles that will help smoothen the page establishment process.

Sell jewelry like a pro with Bijoux.

More info / Download Demo

10. Reprizo

Reprizo is an all-around flexible, customizable and easy to use WordPress theme for pushing jewelry and watches.

Pick from the 5 available demos and benefit from all the other internal pages.

Effortless customization, social media integration, blog, coupons, reviews, header, and footer builder, you name it, Reprizo sports it.

The tool is also congruent with mobile devices and SEO regulations and optimized for fast loading speed. Kick-start your online jewelry business with Reprizo and make a difference.

More info / Download Demo

11. Artemis

Artemis is a multipurpose WordPress theme whose focus is on eCommerce. This theme suits fashion and jewelry industries very well.

It still, however, features WPBakery page builder to make endless customizations. Set up amazing photo galleries and slides with Slider Revolution.

Artemis also comes with multiple demos, like one for jewelry. It features contact pages forms with Ajax, and it is entirely translatable.

You will find WooCommerce integration, a shopping sidebar, wishlists, cart summaries, mini carts, etc.

More info / Download Demo

12. Hongo

If you want to sell jewelry online, Hongo is the spectacular WordPress theme that does the trick. You can also customize Hongo to the last detail with the effortless process of dragging and dropping.

The features of Hongo are efficient and very convenient. From trendy product pages and powerful filtering to a mega menu, 200+ elements and Slider Revolution, Hongo rocks it all.

A killer online presence is just around the corner. Start with the right foot forward and pick Hongo now.

More info / Download Demo

13. CiyaShop

CiyaShop is one fantastic and high-performing jewelry WordPress theme with all the necessary.

While you can use demo homepages and pages out of the box, feel free to tweak it and adjust it. (CiyaShop is ready for any challenge!)

It is a WooCommerce compatible theme with many other premium plugins included. That’s store locator, Instagram store, custom tabs, Amazon affiliate, request form, and product video view.

You are just short moments away from kicking things off on the web and start selling jewelry and other accessories.

More info / Download Demo

14. Jewellery

It is not hard to guess this theme’s niche with a name like Jewelry. It’s designed specifically for eCommerce and reliable website construction.

One of the most popular WP plugins, WooCommerce, facilitates the creation of jaw-dropping online shops.

Upon purchase, customers will benefit from a lifetime of free theme updates. This theme is not a stagnant product, given that it will constantly evolve and improve to keep up with the competition.

More info / Download Demo

15. Sentinal

Sentinal is the WordPress theme that will do the trick when setting up an online jewelry store. You can customize and brand Sentinal to your liking entirely.

It comes with the WPBakery page builder plugin, unlocking complete creative freedom.

Custom header and footer designs, live Ajax search, back-to-top button, social sharing, and mega menu are different things Sentinal has.

Speed and SEO optimization are also part of the deal, mobile readiness and browser compatibility.

More info / Download Demo

16. Prospero

This jewelry WordPress theme is the right pick for fashion, jewelry, or retail venture. Prospero has a modern and easy-to-use WPBakery builder, which welcomes all users due to its friendly and straightforward interface.

Get the Prospero theme and achieve outstanding features and plugins such as YITH compare, YITH wishlist, Mailchimp, Contact Form 7, etc.

The support team is reliable and approachable should you have concerns about this theme. Numerous home designs, different headers and footers, custom shop columns, and many other goodies await every Prospero user.

More info / Download Demo
