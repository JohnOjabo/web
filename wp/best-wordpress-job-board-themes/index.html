14 Best Job Board WordPress Themes & Plugins 2023

  • January 6, 2023
  • Aigars
  • Theme Collections
  • 291 Comments

Today, we would like to present a list of the best job board WordPress themes.

You can use these themes for general job listing websites, niche sites like freelancer job listings, and even job boards for babysitters. They feature front-end submission, location-aware search, resume creators, visual builders, etc.

I have been involved in the recruitment business for 10 years working for several international recruitment companies.

Yup, I know the ins and outs of the industry.

Job portal business is taken by large websites like Glassdoor, Indeed, LinkedIn, and Google Jobs.

However, there are many niches where relatively small websites perform well.

The main problem with small players is to keep the website up to date, error-free and constantly add new functionality requested by users.

This is where WordPress comes into the game. You can easily set up your job listing page and keep it fresh forever.

Below you can find the best job board WordPress themes I found that are powerful and 100% customizable.

Best Job Board WordPress Themes

1. Jobify

Jobify is a modern flat design-inspired WordPress theme designed for job boards. It features a famous 5-minute install method to get your website up and running.

It’s a customizable theme with unlimited color and font variations and other options to tweak your website.

This is my favorite theme on this list, and its incredible popularity shows that 15,000 users agree with me.

Price: $69

Get started with Jobify today.

2. Jobseek

For those who wish to create a professional job board website, Jobseek is an ideal choice. This versatile WordPress theme has a charming design and an intuitive interface.

The Admin UI will showcase jobs in a very organized manner. You will find it easy to work with Jobseek.

Employers may post available jobs on the page’s front-end and display information like location.

It is also possible to preview posts before publishing. The prolific WooCommerce plugin enables the paid listing function. The client will easily set up their website, and there is no need to tamper with the code.

A nifty testimonial management system allows customers to review your business and share their opinion regarding the service.

Price: $39

Get started with Jobseek today.

3. WorkScout

WorkScout is an excellent WordPress theme for job board websites. It is great for any employment agency recruiter due to its extreme user-friendliness.

Page construction is intuitive, thanks to the WPBakery page builder plugin. This amazing tool uses drag and drop technology to construct a website fast.

An informative online documentation source is available for novice users. It explains every feature in detail, offering to guide you through the setup process.

The Resume Manager tool makes it easier for users to register and upload their resumes.

Site owners can upgrade to using WC Paid Listings. This makes it easier to implement paid listings and turns any website into a fully-formed online job market. WorkScout makes things happen!

Price: $79

Get started with WorkScout today.

4. JobCareer

JobCareer is a brilliant solution for webmasters to establish a modern and functional job board website.

This theme comes with helpful plugins and amazing, professional-quality template pages and websites that make initial website creation, design and setup an intuitive, breezy matter accomplished through a drag-and-drop interface.

Capacities such as geolocated job searches, job, and email alerts, or application deadlines make JobCareer amazingly flexible in its reach.

Free job submissions and a fully operational WooCommerce payment system are included, too.

Social app integration is smoothly executed with exclusive buttons and icons, while helpful application tracking capabilities let users stay on top of their job offers and bids. Start building your job board website today with JobCareer!

Price: $69

Get started with JobCareer today.

5. Recruitment Agency

Recruitment Agency is a responsive, fast loading and adaptable WordPress theme built on Bootstrap. It fits businesses and individuals that need their profile exposed.

It is a congruent mix between a directory and an application system. Recruitment Agency divides pages in listings according to what you look for to hire or apply.

It has unlimited portfolios and blog layouts. Files such as CV or resume, support documents and such, can be uploaded in different formats.

Recruitment agency is developer-friendly; it doesn’t require coding knowledge to set.

It uses the amazing Essential Grid to arrange sections, maps for job locations, Slider Revolution, etc. There are no limits with Recruitment Agency!

Price: $64

Get started with Recruitment Agency today.

6. Cariera

Cariera is an expert on job boards, stats, and listings. Try it out with 12 homepages and a one-click demo import. Cariera is built with tons of premium quality plugins such as WPBakery builder. (Easily customizable!)

Furthermore, Cariera is a theme for almost any scenario that a work environment might throw. It counts with WooCommerce and WPML for purchase and translation aspects, respectively.

Cariera serves as the perfect place for companies and job aspirants to connect. It also offers job search filters and CV submissions, uses Contact Form 7 to build forms, and has a child theme.

Create the best environment for job/employers search with Cariera!

Price: $79

Get started with Cariera today.

It comes with customizable elements on builder, video backgrounds, 7+ homepages, and parallax.

You get WPBakery Page Builder, unlimited sidebars, Web Font Icons, and Slider Revolution to set up slides.

Injob gives you handcrafted options such as front admin reviews or email notifications. It also gives employer management for apps.

It has incredible panel/dashboard options for both companies to professional individuals. Injob is also connected to social media and even offers social network login.

This tool is thought for the best practical option to increase employment chances.

Price: $59

Get started with Injob today.

Customers can expect no less than full accessibility, with no programming knowledge or third-party support required.

WordPress Job Board Plugins

1. WP Job Board (Plugin)

WP Job Board is built with a wide range of capabilities. It is easy to install and can be added to new or established websites.

What’s more, extensive and informative documentation is available, explaining everything you need to know. Also, you may contact the plugin’s support staff via email with a <24 hrs response time.

3 main hurdles stop people from creating job boards, and this plugin has eliminated all of them: It is low-cost, easy-to-use, and user-friendly!

Undoubtedly versatile, this plugin proves to be very effective. WordPress Job Board plays well with other 3rd-party services such as Stripe and Paypal. A detailed review of this plugin is available here.

Price: $97

Get started with WP Job Board today.

2. Recruitly Addons

Recruitly Addons is an exclusive collection of tools for recruitment and job listings. This plugin works with Elementor page builder, so you don’t incur any inconvenience.

You get all sorts of practical specialties that will expand your website with unique new features.

From job lists, search and compare to favorites, recruiters, and popular jobs display, all this and more is what Recruitly Addons brings to the table.

You will also never need to touch a single string of code when using Recruitly Addons and all the goodies that come with it.

Price: $24

Get started with Recruitly today.

3. WP Job Manager (Plugin)

WP Job Manager is a powerful, easy-to-use job board plugin compatible with most WordPress themes.

It is a highly customizable and flexible platform with various payment and listing options.

There are numerous add-ons available to further extend its functionality with custom resume pages, different payment integration, paid listings, social login, etc.

Are you looking to create a professional-looking job board? Then WP Job Manager is the right solution!
