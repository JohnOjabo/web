20 Best WordPress Video Themes For Video Blogs and Vlogs 2023

We’re excited to bring you this collection of carefully picked WordPress video themes for excellent content distribution.

Due to increasing competition, standing out from other industry professionals has become insanely difficult.

Once you get more serious about videography it might be the right time to create a unique and professional website to showcase your work.

This will get you more leads and other business opportunities as videographer, filmmaker, or hobby video capturer.

Here are themes that accept front-end submission, themes for professional film studios, and others to host your videos or embed them from Youtube, Vimeo and other popular sources.

Best WordPress Themes For Videos

Let’s look at the best video WordPress themes you can install today.

01. MagPlus

MagPlus is a responsive WordPress video blog and magazine website theme. It is the best platform to craft sleek, compelling new websites.

Personal and professional blogs, online magazines and more look great with MagPlus. Getting things started is quick and simple. 40+ custom templates and infinite layouts offer impressive flexibility.

MagPlus lets you fiddle with 150+ elements in endless ways. Video-centric websites, in particular, look crisp and load fast with MagPlus.

You don’t need to write a line of code to get professional quality results with MagPlus.

This theme comes with a completely visual interface that is friendly for beginners. MagPlus is ready for your videos.

02. Soledad

Soledad is a flexible and easy-to-use WordPress video theme for video magazine-style websites.

It has been constructed with an ingenious set of creative tools, premium plugins, and a huge collection of gorgeous demo websites.

Soledad is the theme for those who need to be heard! Personal and professional bloggers prefer Soledad for its clean aesthetic, seamless navigation experience and powerful plugins, such as the Penci Recipe Plugin and the Penci Review Plugin for food lovers and foodie bloggers outright critics alike.

However, you can build your website however you are most comfortable with WPBakery.

7,000+ included template and demo variations ensure you create things how you want.

03. VideoPro

VideoPro is a premium quality specialized video WordPress theme. It has many specific features such as playlists, channels, live broadcasts, etc.

This theme uses BuddyPress for the interaction and follow-ups of viewers. It also features an awesome Membership Plugin by WPMU integration for memberships and subscriptions.

VideoPro, like mobile YouTube, lets you watch videos while searching for content. Users will also get monthly updates and bug fixes aside from customer support.

With this theme, you will integrate the best features on the market. VideoPro lets users download content (depending on the admin choice) through simple buttons.

Start using this packed-up amazing theme now!

04. PenNews

PenNews is a modern and responsive video WordPress theme for magazine-style websites.

With sophisticated features, PenNews affords every blog the trappings of pro websites. Dazzle audiences with tailored demos and polished templates. Deploy a wealth of content widgets with WPBakery.

Google AMP and Facebook Instant Articles keep server loads light across social media. Awesome video sliders, galleries, and portfolios showcase your visual content in a flattering light.

Perfect SEO drives traffic to your website without breaking a sweat. Auto-translation features bring your whole website to new audiences in minutes.

Reach across language barriers and let your videos go viral.

05. Vlogger

Vlogger is a professional tutorial and video WordPress theme. If you are a YouTuber or make video blogs, this theme is for you. If you make online courses or video tutorials, you are at home.

Your passion does not matter: photography, food, travels or cars can work with it.

Enjoy adding slideshows on every possible video format too. It is mobile-first; hence, fast and responsive.

Make contact with customers by adding your own MailChimp newsletter!

You also count on 12 ad slots to monetize your videos. Place ads everywhere, including a fly-out ad in whichever design you want. Vlogger is well documented to guide you through all the creative processes.

If your business is related to videos, get Vlogger now!

Vlog is a unique and beautiful video WordPress theme on top of its game for video blogging, vlogging, video makers and related, similar websites.

It auto-detects video thumbnails and displays them as the header of a video post. Or it can group your videos in playlists for your convenience.

Vlog seamlessly handles all the modern video sharing platforms and is compatible with many more, including YouTube, Vimeo, DailyMotion, and Twitch, making different niche markets more easily reached.

In contrast, 200+ post layout combinations ensure your Vlog is always the faithful presentation of your vision.

07. VidoRev

Creating a video website with VidoRev theme and WordPress is a piece of cake. No matter your level of technical skill, like coding and designing, VidoRev lifts it to level eleven.

Even if you are new to the game, this convenient WordPress video theme allows beginners to create professional video review pages without a single drop of sweat.

You can create niche or generic video pages with a web design that caters to your heart’s content to a T.

Moreover, you can also create a membership area, continuously grow your loyal community, and take your online project idea to an entirely new degree.

08. Newspaper

Newspaper is a beautiful and famous video theme designed to make your website stand out.

It comes with an intuitive interface and a responsive design. Fully integrated with YouTube, Vimeo and Dailymotion, this theme allows you to create video posts and display them on your website’s homepage, pages, mega menus, sidebars, grids, categories, footer, etc.

Moreover, this theme can handle large amounts of content and has 120+ unique demos. You can easily change your website’s look and feel from the powerful and intuitive Theme Panel area, you can easily change your website’s look & feel without any coding skills required.

Newspaper Theme also has an intelligent ad system to make your website AdSense ready in a few seconds.

Create the most immersive experience for your visitors!

09. Jannah

Jannah is a friendly WordPress theme for video-sharing lovers.

It makes your content mobile-optimized and load fast everywhere with the AMP project.

Moreover, Jannah has perfect match features like Video Playlists. Building Video Playlists for users is a great way to keep them longer.

It offers a beautifully fluid block with a scrolling list that is easy to access and intuitive. You will find a style that pleases your interest quickly with 20+ ready-to-use demos.

Use the one-click demo import feature to get your design. From there, you need to update and customize as you wish. Simple, fast, complete – Jannah is waiting for you.

Videobox is a visually aesthetic WordPress theme designed to show video content.

You will get compatibility for YouTube, Vimeo and more. It uses WooCommerce for all eCommerce related things. Videobox has special features such as automatic video thumbnails and video tabs.

Some of these work for categories like most popular and recent posts. You will have full decision and liberty on which posts to display. Social media connection for Facebook and Instagram is also provided.

Videobox is also a perfect theme for online streaming, news channels, or magazines. Paparazzi and bloggers will love it!

Additionally, Videobox offers multiple headers, sidebars and widget areas. You get a carousel style, and colors are unlimited and customizable.

Silverscreen is a striking WordPress video theme for movies, filmmakers, production companies, even vloggers.

You get several different demos that you can take to your benefit right of the get-go. With Silverscreen, you can start on the internet extremely fast.

You do not even need to touch a single line of code to be able to make improvements to Silverscreen.

You mix and match the available, insert your branding, and have a fully functional page ready to roll.

Silverscreen is your best bet to create a professional and impactful website for everything video-related.

Vidio is a practical and easy-to-use video manager WordPress theme for starting your online project quickly and effortlessly. Select the demo that resonates with you best and go from there.
