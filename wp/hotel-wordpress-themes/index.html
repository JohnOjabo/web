23 Best Hotel WordPress Themes With Online Booking 2023

Here, you will find the best hotel WordPress themes with a booking system for any hotel size.

We knew what to look for in themes since we worked with a few hotel owners on their websites. Thus, we removed 25+ since they did not meet the requirements.

But these 23 do!

The following collection gives you many designs to build a unique hotel website.

You can easily adjust WordPress themes via the intuitive, user-friendly interface. As a result, even a person unfamiliar with coding will be comfortable changing the design the way they prefer.

It’s so effortless.

Plus, you can examine each theme individually by clicking “demo.” The first-hand experience is so fantastic.

Unleash the best online hotel presentation EVER with these WordPress themes.

In hurry? Here are the four best hotel WordPress themes in 2023

Best Hotel WordPress Themes

1. Bellevue

Bellevue is a beautiful WordPress bed & breakfast and hotel website theme.

The tool uses the most advanced web development technologies available. It’s a framework of powerful features that make website design and development an easy, breezy matter.

Bellevue has a functional availability checking calendar that updates in real-time.

It has a versatile payment system powered by the WooCommerce eCommerce plugin, and ready-to-go online shop pages to directly market your packages or services, products, and wares.

Bellevue will always look how you want it to, with tons of customization options in the intuitive WPBakery page builder.

2. Villagio 

The Villagio rental WordPress theme is crafted for the fastest reservation process and bookings management, thanks to integration with the Hotel Booking plugin integration. 

This plugin ensures the most advanced booking functionality. Hotel Booking supports a real-time availability calendar that synchronizes with OTAs like Booking or Airbnb. It offers advanced search & sorting options, a custom rates system, automatic confirmation, discount coupons, tax reports etc.

Villagio features a concise and stylish design, which suits any type of accommodation rental, from villas and B&Bs to apartments. Its compatible with all popular page builders and the Gutenberg editor.

More info / Download Demo

3. Luviana

Luviana is optimized for instant hotel and property rental bookings through the MotoPress Hotel Booking system that is included in Luviana WordPress template for free.

Except stylish design, this vacation rental template offers unique booking options. It enables site owners to accept and manage payments from guests via all the most popular getaways and provides real-time property search with the ability to make custom attributes to search forms for better navigation.

The other highlights of Hotel Booking are multiple booking confirmation modes, the ability to upsell extra services, set custom rates, edit guest accounts and much more. The theme is optimized for Gutenberg and integrated with a library of 40+ Getwid blocks.

4 Hoteller | Hotel Booking WordPress

To help your business grow, grabbing one of the best WordPress themes for hotels is a way to win. Clean and responsive, Hoteller looks great on any modern device.

It also features tons of unique features such as seasonal pricing, booking management, flexible rates, etc.

Besides, allow your visitors to pay through multiple payment gateways.

As a result, you can maximize your sales potential, and your bottom line will be glad you did.

5. LeadEngine

LeadEngine is a clean, crafty, and responsive WordPress multipurpose theme.

A wealth of demos, template pages, and blocks provide functionality and choice, coding-free. Advanced customization settings let you brand your website within minutes.

LeadEngine’s Hotel demo gives you everything you need to start immediately.

Showcase rooms, packages, and offerings in style with minimalist portfolios. Beautiful Slider Revolution leads your customers where you want them to go.

Efficient coding ensures peak performance under all traffic conditions. It’s also fully SEO optimized. Take your hotel business website to the next level with LeadEngine!

6. Kalium

Clean and trendy with a minimal touch, Kalium is one of the best hotel WordPress themes with an incredible design. It will spark their curiosity and make them want to learn more about your place, rooms, and services.

Create a compelling offer, mix it with Kalium’s incredible look, and they will not be able to resist. What’s best, the majority of work is already done for you.

Aside from the included hotel demo, Kalium has other features and benefits. Awesome typography options, Slider Revolution, WooCommerce compatible, and 100% RTL and WPML ready, it is all there.

Kalium is also optimized for SEO and speed and allows you to implement content marketing using the blog section. Whatever you would like to attain, reach it quicker with Kalium.

7. Hotello

Hotello is one of the best hotel WordPress themes with an incredible design for your accommodation business.

You can use it for all sorts of businesses, like hotels, motels, bed and breakfast, resorts, apartments, you name it. For a quick solution, Hotello comes with 5 stunning and eye-catchy demos!

Hotello offers 3 booking modes: entirely translatable, multilingual, and 100% GDPR compliant. What’s more? It also supports online and offline payments, seasonal prices, and is equipped with email notifications.

Spice things up with parallax or video background, implement real-time weather widget, and benefit from AMP readiness. Hotello is a cracking WP theme to boost your hotel business.

8. Kinsley

Kinsley is a fully-featured hotel WordPress theme that requires no experience to master. It comes with multiple front and internal page layouts, saving you a ton of time.

Mix and match! Then use Elementor page builder to perform customizations, and that’s it.

Moreover, Kinsley also has a complete online booking management system, keeping your business flowing smoothly.

Kinsley comes with everything: online and offline payments, availability calendar, automatic syncing with 3rd-party platforms, and GDPR compliance. (You can even create an online store with WooCommerce.)

Your hotel deserves the best – Go Kinsley!

With Hotel WP, you can build your hotel, apartment rental, or any other accommodation business website.

It follows all the latest technologies to ensure your page runs smoothly on all devices and platforms.
