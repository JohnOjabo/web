What to look for in directory WordPress themes

  • Robust and powerful structure that can handle many listings, especially when entering a big industry or even starting a generic directory platform. Once familiar with your directory, you can quickly have thousands of submissions to review and publish.
  • Mobile compatibility: Browsing products, services, locations, whatever, is highly rewarding if you can do it from your favorite mobile device. Of course, all these high-quality WordPress themes ensure a fluid layout that instantly adapts to any screen size.
  • Easy setup: With a WordPress theme, you can experience a speedy setup that will take you just a few clicks. In short, kick-start your directory project and work on modifications and additional tweaks immediately.
  • Admin/user dashboards: For awesome website management and a great user experience, a powerful admin and a practical user dashboard are necessary. You get the best of both with resourceful alternatives, so you don’t need to sort anything out yourself.
  • Search function: Unless it is super niche, every directory website needs a well-thought-out search function, preferably with advanced options. This allows the user to find what they are searching for much quicker. These themes also have great navigability and can break things down into different categories.
  • Plans: Whether starting a free directory website, a premium one or both, make sure you present pricing plans and have the full members’ area sorted out. Do you need to do it yourself? Of course not; that’s when a theme comes into play.
  • Forms: Another essential feature of a directory/listing WordPress themes are forms, primarily for submissions.
  • Reviews: Focus on credibility with reviews and ratings, as this will help users identify who to trust and work with. Reviews often come integrated into the web design, but on all other occasions, you are welcome to introduce them via a plugin.
  • Make money: There are numerous ways how these WordPress themes allow you to earn money with your directory websites. Advertising, premium bundles and subscriptions, listing submissions, appointment booking, and more.
  • Payment gateways: different payment gateways come included with the WordPress theme bundle (PayPal, Stripe, etc.).
