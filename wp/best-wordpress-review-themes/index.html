13 Best WordPress Review Themes 2023

  • February 3, 2023
  • Aigars
  • Theme Collections
  • 51 Comments

You better not miss our complete collection of the top WordPress review themes if you’re good at reviewing stuff.

People want to hear your opinion!

We did a lot of reviewing and testing over the past months to make this list happen.

So you can now enjoy only the best options to confidently start your review website.

All the themes are for blogs and online magazines with an optional review system. (Plus Schema compatible review system)

Moreover, these themes are great for affiliate marketers, too.

Best WordPress Review Themes (Kickstart Your Affiliate Website!)

1. MagPlus

MagPlus is a WordPress review theme with 40+ demos to choose from. (It has something for EVERYONE!)

On top of that, with the user-friendly interface, you are also set to launch a new review website swiftly. By the way, all the editing, designing, and customizing happen visually.

You will never need to touch a single code line when working with MagPlus. Managing and maintaining a thriving review website will be a breeze.

Moreover, MagPlus comes with twelve header layouts, 150+ custom-made elements, 5 different post layouts, and 20 sliders.

Do some mixing and matching and have a complete and functional page ready for the launch fast.

More info / Download Demo

2. Magic

Magic is a clean and modern WordPress theme that you can use for a news, magazine, or review website.

It’s built with clean and valid HTML5 and CSS3 to ensure that your website will KEEP UP with the newest trends in web design.

Magic comes with a drag and drop builder that lets you easily create a unique page layout for your website.

It has a smart theme options panel that allows you to enable, disable and edit almost every element.

One of the highlights of this theme is its built-in review system that comes with different review criteria such as stars, percentages, and points.

Moreover, it includes rich snippet review Microdata to enhance your search result and improve your websites online visibility. This theme is also optimized for SEO and ensures a great mobile experience.

More info / Download

3. MagOne

MagOne is a colorful WordPress product reviews theme. Compose the most professional and polished online magazines and blogs for any purpose, market, or niche.

With MagOnes powerful Sheet Framework, customize your unique pages and websites from scratch or base on MagOnes powerful pre-designed template pages.

Advanced theme options let you do anything with the look and feel of your MagOne magazine or blog.

Professional quality widgets and shortcodes are readily available within MagOne or as compatible 3rd-party additions, expanding the possibilities IMMENSELY.

And with a cutting-edge mega menu, unique headers and footers, and complete WPML translation compatibility, MagOne is the theme to beat!

More info / Download Demo

4. Newsmag

Newsmag is a popular WordPress theme that lets you efficiently write and publish reviews online.

The vast white space in this theme makes a huge room to HIGHLIGHT your post and turn your audience’s attention to your content.

The smooth homepage design allows you to display featured content, the latest post for various categories, and other content in widget areas.

The creators bundled it with the WPBakery page builder plugin, a powerful drag and drop page builder to create unlimited page layouts for your website.

Give visitors an amazing user experience and make them hungry to return for more.

More info / Download

5. Gridlove

Gridlove is a magazine and classical writing-oriented WordPress theme. You will get it with a focus on aesthetic marketing appeal and good writing props. Perfect for reviews!

It is ready for business in general with the WooCommerce plugin. For news, communities, threads and such, you get bbPress.

The developers also optimized it for speed, making its performance great and smooth.

Gridlove offers unlimited sidebars, infinite scrolling, and multi-page posts. You will also find it completely adaptable to all screens and browsers.

Moreover, they made 12 SPECIAL shortcodes available for quick integration/expansion.

Constant updates arrive with easy access on the dashboard. Try this awesome writing instrument now.

More info / Download Demo

6. Valenti

Valenti is a clean, modern cutting-edge WordPress review magazine theme designed and developed for DEMANDING users.

It’s a highly customizable WordPress theme with a simple drag & drop page builder, unlimited color variations, and much more.

This theme also features a modern parallax scrolling effect, which no other theme can offer. Moreover, the developers continually update this theme, releasing improvements and upgrades.

Additionally, this theme supports the WooCommerce toolkit and BuddyPress to easily build your community and eCommerce website.

Start your review website with confidence!

More info / Demo

7. Puzzles

Puzzles is an up-to-date WordPress theme that unlocks many new options and possibilities. Installing the theme on your website won’t look like other websites.

Make it stand out from the crowd!

It is also a highly customizable WordPress theme with unlimited color variations, a grid-style layout, and an integrated review system.

Moreover, it is designed using Metro UI principles. You can find other flat and Metro design inspired themes here.

Start online like a BOSS.

More info / Demo

8. Sahifa

Sahifa is a clean, beautiful and versatile WordPress theme designed for magazines, news, blogs, review or any other website. Its also a 100% editable theme with tons of useful features.

This includes an advanced review system, translation, and multilingual ready core. Sahifa is optimized to work with bbPress, BuddyPress, WooCommerce, and WPML, and has already been translated into several languages. (Saves extra time!)

This is the best-selling WordPress magazine theme with 28,000+ purchases and the number keeps on growing.

BOOM.

More info / Demo

9. Suga

Whether you would like your review site to have a structure of a blog or an online magazine, Suga is the VERSATILE tool that will help you on your journey.

With the included and ready-to-use demo material, you can have a page all set without sweat. By employing the BKNinja Composer, you can modify Suga by dragging and dropping.

Suga practices all the trends of the current web. It is responsive, SEO-ready, fast loading, and compatible with popular browsers.

Suga’s additional treats are mega menu, custom colors, sticky header and sidebar, social sharing, and integrated review system.

Start with the right foot forward and enjoy a PLEASANT site-building experience with the stunning Suga.

More info / Download Demo

10. Gorgo

If you are looking to dive into different niches, but would like to have a page built for each, Gorgo is the tool you need. And if you would like to create a general website, you can do that, too!

Have in mind, Gorgo is also perfect for advanced web developers who are looking for something more.

Loads of different layouts, dark and light mode, striking loading speed, tons of plugins and search engine friendliness, Gorgo is here to take care of all your desires and needs.

More info / Download Demo

11. Gazek

Gazek is an exclusive review WordPress theme with an additional membership area to build loyal online communities. The theme is modern and eye-catchy, offering you to create a striking and impactful website for any type of review website.

While the webmaster can add their reviews, Gazek is a community-focused theme for encouraging others to leave a review/feedback on different services, products, locations, etc.

You can start working with Gazek RIGHT AWAY with the one-click demo data installation.

Other goodies are Slider Revolution, WPBakery page builder, Contact Form 7, parallax effect, and scrolling animations.

More info / Download Demo

12. Puva

Puva is a WordPress theme that combines affiliate products and reviews exceptionally well. If this is the business model you want to dive into, make sure you do not miss Puva.

It is a superb solution that simplifies the process while keeping professionalism at the highest degree.

In the kit, you get 3 demos, RTL layout, custom widgets for Elementor, newsletter subscription, and unlimited creative options.

Moreover, Puva is also fully translatable, so you can easily localize your affiliate reviews website.

Start your online business with a SOLID foundation that always operates without a hitch.

More info / Download Demo

13. Networker

Networker is an all-around online magazine WordPress theme that you will find perfect for starting a review site.

You receive many fully customizable front and internal pages with the one-click installation method.

Other specialties of Networker are dark mode toggle, AMP compatibility, lazy loading, AdSense integration and live search, to name a few.

Social integration, opt-in forms, multiple-authors, and advanced reviews are all the other amazing amenities that make Networker truly special.

Enjoy the process and create a powerful reviews page with Networker now.
