You are in the right place if you are looking for WordPress bbPress forum themes!

We like bbPress A LOT and even use it for our theme support forum. (We know you’ll like it too.)

It’s simple: bbPress is the perfect choice for WordPress users!

But instead of integrating it manually, get a theme that does that for you out-of-the-box.

Especially if you’re in the starting phase.

We only collected themes compatible with the latest WordPress version and popular plugins, so you get the best solution and user experience for your visitors.

Let’s look at a list of the best WordPress forum and community themes based on bbPress that suit everyone.

Best WordPress Forum Themes

Note: A forum section can help boost your web presence, strengthen your online brand, and establish relationships with people within your niche or industry.

1. BuddyBoss

BuddyBoss is the most advanced yet easy-to-use forum WordPress theme for any category.

Furthermore, BuddyBoss allows you to personalize your social network (BuddyPress) regardless of your skill level. No coding or programming knowledge is required.

You can customize the widgets, logos, cover photos, and navigation. This theme incorporates inputs, buttons, tabs, tooltips, and progress bars.

BuddyBoss is translation-ready able to display your content in any language. (Multilingual and RTL support.)

Your website will have live notifications like other pages that focus on interaction. You will not be required to refresh your page to stay updated constantly.

Overall, this is one of the best BuddyPress/WordPress themes at an affordable price.

2. Sway

If a bbPress forum is what you are looking to build, make it happen with ease and confidence, thanks to Sway. You do not need any other tools and extensions, as Sway takes care of it out of the box.

What’s also cool, you can use Sway for nearly any website you want and then expand it with a community section, using the ready-to-use sample.

Additionally, if you would like to translate Sway, that’s an option due to WPML and Polylang compatibility.

3. LeadEngine

LeadEngine is a serious deal for the best and most refined WordPress forum theme based on bbPress. It’s a multi-purpose theme with a page builder to build any website.

Not only does LeadEngine have a full demo at your disposal, but you can also completely customize it however you fancy.

No matter how hard you go on the web design, adjusting-wise, LeadEngine makes sure that the end product always works seamlessly across all platforms.

Kick things off in style, encourage people to participate, and grow your community with LeadEngine.

4. Ekko

Ekko is a multi-purpose WordPress forum theme with unlimited options and possibilities. You can kick off any page you want and easily add a forum.

On the other hand, create a full-blown forum that you can later expand with a blog and an online store.

The design of Ekko is super clean and minimalistic with excellent attention to detail. It also includes social media icons, a customizable sidebar, footer reveals function, and different widgets.

Default settings of Ekko include speed and SEO optimization, too.

5. KLEO

KLEO is an exceptionally versatile forum WordPress theme whose design meets all your needs for a modern theme.

It doesn’t matter whether you are a professional or an amateur – you can easily customize this theme!

To be specific, KLEO incorporates a responsive and user-friendly admin interface. With these advanced features, You can add and remove elements and configure the default look. (Do. It. Your. Way.)

Another advantage of KLEO is the WPBakery page builder that doesn’t require coding to build a professional website.

Of course, it also supports the BuddyPress and bbPress plugins for building a forum quickly.

Olympus is a fantastic WordPress bbPress forum theme that helps you quick-start your community and/or social network. (Avoid hiring a coder, designer to create a web platform from scratch.)

When you see how much pre-designed content Olympus brings, you will instantly understand how simple it is to create a forum and start a community.

There are tons of fantastic options that you can take to your advantage for creating a fully functional social network.

Start your thing with Olympus, from mobile profiles, comments, login pages, video albums, post reactions, and all the rest in between.

It only takes a click to install the demo and start your successful journey.

With its dark and bold look, SquadForce ensures a striking appearance that will blow everyone away. Whether you have any or no experience, you will witness a spectacular result in a little breeze with SquadForce.

Before we dive in further, understand that Cera follows all the trends and regulations of the modern web.

You will find everything necessary to start on the web like a pro in the enormous bundle of features.

Turn your idea into a reality with the superb Cera.

Docly is a WordPress bbPress forum theme for creating a knowledge base website, even product documentation.

With the many great page layouts and elements, you have multiple options and possibilities to tailor the end product to your liking. There is no need for coding work when considering performing improvements.

A few of Docly’s highlights are RTL support, dark and light mode, PSD files, sticky elements, and carousels, to name a few.

Support and documentation are also part of the kit, so you will never feel lonely on your journey to successful website realization.

Creating your social network happens quicker than you think with the right tools – You only need Vikinger!

This WordPress theme takes care of all the heavy lifting for you, so you only focus on refining touches, and that’s it. The list of features is endless, guaranteeing everyone gets the most out of Vikinger.

Profiles, badges, reactions, rankings, bbPress forum, private groups, Twitch integration, GamiPress, and blog module are only goodies with Vikinger.

Zippco is an all-around WordPress theme for finance consulting with an option to expand it with a bbPress forum. The tool has several ready-made homes, internal pages, and stunning header styles.

You can create the exact web space you desire without the hard work with Zippco.

Other treats of Zippco contain custom sidebars, boxed layout, child theme, online appointments, unlimited colors, Slider Revolution, and social media integration.

If you want to start a video-focused website, Netube is the right solution. Plus, you can also use it as a WordPress bbPress forum theme!

Tessera is a WordPress bbPress forum theme for the knowledge base, help desk, and online support web platform.

Even if using the default settings of Tessera, you’ll make a crazy good website.

Still, if you would like to fine-tune the layout, do it at free will, as Tessera supports all customization tweaks you’d like to perform. (Thanks, dragging and dropping technology!)

14. Church and Events

Church and Events is a WordPress theme specially designed for churches and events.

This fully responsive theme is ideal for inexperienced web developers and inexperienced users.

The theme includes different features to help create an awesome website for church events. It has a pixel-perfect design, CSS and parallax effects, large-sized elements, scrolling effects, and metro-style icons and fonts on each page.

Make theme customization fast and easy with a drag-and-drop page builder, shortcodes, unlimited colors and font options, and useful plugins. It also includes WooCommerce and bbPress (duh!).

Church and Events also support the Event Calendar Pro and Community Events to keep everyone updated.

15. Vellum

Vellum is a flexible WordPress theme that is perfect for any website. It’s a modern theme with so much potential as it is fully responsive, SEO-friendly, and easy to customize.

Vellum’s distinctive structure allows you to create a unique layout design for your website. It incorporates wonderful features, including a drag-and-drop page composer, UberMenu mega menus, parallax backgrounds, and more.

Its powerful slider plugin gives your website and slideshows an extra appeal that will surely draw the attention of your target visitors or readers.

One of Impreza’s best features is its strong support for bbPress integration.
