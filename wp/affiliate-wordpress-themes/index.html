13 Best Affiliate Marketing WordPress Themes To Make Money Online 2023

Earning commissions happens easier with these tested and proven affiliate WordPress themes.

You don’t need to focus on building the right marketing website because these themes cover it all out of the box.

It’s all more or less about picking the one that suits your business best, although the first 3 are OUR favorites. (But you can’t make the wrong pick!)

You can now make a niche or general affiliate site that feels trustworthy.

High quality is all that matters, precisely what you get. So enjoy every feature and element you need to start in the affiliate industry like a CHAMP (quickly!).

Cha-ching.

Best WordPress Themes for Affiliate Marketing

1. MagPlus

MagPlus is a multipurpose tool that will make you stand out from other affiliate marketing websites!

It comes with 40+ demos oriented to the news and communication industry. You will find a way to deliver whatever message you need to send properly.

Moreover, MagPlus is visually appealing and customized to avoid coding (hurray!). It uses a mega menu to ease site navigation.

MagPlus is also a fast and adaptable tool that perfectly adjusts to screens and devices. Furthermore, the developers integrated MagPlus with 3rd-party plugins such as Yellow Pencil, WooCommerce, and Jumbo Pack.

You get only the best!

More info / Download Demo

2. Jevelin(Most Popular)

Jevelin is a potent and pliable WordPress multipurpose theme for any affiliate website.

It combines full-fledged demo websites with dozens of elements and features.

You will also find a sophisticated drag and drop page builder within. That is how Jevelin puts you in control of your website’s visual detailing and its behavior. (Customization is as easy!)

You get many features to style your affiliate website with custom headers, footers, pagination, slideshows, parallax effect, etc.

Jevelin is a well-rounded package that fits any industry without a hitch. GO.

More info / Download Demo

3. Gillion (Trending Item)

Gillion is a WordPress theme made for affiliate marketers and alike. It uses WPBakery as the main page builder and a powerful interface. It is also highly responsive and flexible, adapting to all screens and browsers.

Gillion is oriented to magazines, blogs and writing or publishing. You will also find 15+ pre-built demos of edgy designs and many blog layouts.

The layout comes with many customizations to play with. (You don’t need any coding skills to use this theme!)

Gillion has unlimited color mixes, several headers, and even an elegant top bar. Make posting in 6 different formats quick and easy! Take your affiliate project to the next level with Gillion.

More info / Download Demo

4. Webify

If you want to push other companies products and earn a commission, you can do that with Webify. We consider it among the best affiliate marketing WordPress themes.

One of the best ways is to build a blog, niche or general, and start growing an audience of loyal followers. You can easily monetize your page with affiliate links, turning your online journal into a money machine.

Webify includes all the suitable material to get you going in no time.

Import the sample with a single click, and you can start turning the default layout into a design that you would like to sport effectively. It’s as easy as that!

More info / Download Demo

5. Genesis Framework

The Genesis theme is an accessible, intuitive WordPress theme for affiliate marketets that enables everyone to quickly and effortlessly build professional looking websites.

Genesis is the most popular theme for Amazon and other affiliates because of its simplicity and flexibility.

You only need minimal skills to implement the Genesis Framework theme on any WordPress website.

And you will instantly be rewarded with many tools and choices to alter the look and feel of your incipient website to match your desires.

More info / Download Demo

6. Marketing Pro

Marketing Pro is an affiliate marketing WordPress theme with dozens of demos for a quick start. It is SEO-friendly, responsive, and lightweight, ensuring the best user experience!

Moreover, Marketing Pro has a minimalistic concept ideal to put your touch and ideas in it. You can build anything, either a regular business or landing page.

In addition, Marketing Pro uses WPBakery page builder and is packed with Slider Revolution. It also features WPML for translation and Google Fonts for typography.

Lastly, it is well documented and provides support, social media buttons, and lifetime updates!

Start your promotion business, giving it the best start-up quality!

More info / Download Demo

7. MagOne

MagOne is a robust and reliable WordPress news and magazine theme. It is built to put together list-style affiliate marketing blogs quickly and effortlessly.

A great amount of demo website templates are into the Sheeit Framework. It powers this robust theme and empowers you to customize layouts or craft pages from scratch within minutes.

It uses a drag and drop interface. Completely ready for all sorts of ad revenue system integration. MagOne features interactive ad blocks to easily monetize your online mag or blog.

The creators included amazing mega menus with MagOne for your convenience, instantly organizing your content for your viewers’ delight.

More info / Download Demo

8. Blabber

Blabber is a fully flexible WordPress theme that you can utilize for different ideas and intentions.

It unlocks unlimited possibilities with the integration of the Elementor page builder.

With nifty front-page demos, you also get several inner layouts that you can mix, match, and enrich with your creative touch. The Blabber’s structure is 100% flexible, easily reshaping any screen.

Other specialties include Slider Revolution, social media integration, header and footer builder, search engine optimization, and interactive Ajax search. You can now start your thing and spread the word out professionally.

More info / Download Demo

9. Netube

Netube is a little different yet still a superb affiliate WordPress theme alternative. It is an excellent solution if you would like your blog or online magazine to focus primarily on video content.

Netube offers a speedy and straightforward realization of a fresh new project with 5 demos. Instead of doing all the heavy lifting yourself, let Netube take care of it.

You only focus on the finishing touches. (Quick and code-free installation and setup.)

Netube is a fast loading, optimized for SEO, mobile-ready and cross-browser compatible.

You can also translate the theme and localize the experience. If you are king at video content, start an online hub with Netube!

More info / Download Demo

10. iffiliate

iffiliate is a very flexible WordPress theme you can use for all affiliate products via WooCommerce. You can also use the theme for Amazon, eBay and Aliexpress, to name a few.

You can start working on your online business confidently with many designs, features and components.

Some goodies of iffiliate are testimonials, contact forms, CSS animations, pop-up login and live search functions.

Due to the drag and drop technology, you can also quickly improve without writing code. Get your affiliate business up and running with iffiliate.

More info / Download Demo

11. Tokoo

Multiply your earnings by attracting potential partners. You can do this with Tokoo, an affiliate WordPress theme that not only specializes in electronics, but you can also use it for dropshipping (even multi-vendor markeplaces).

Tokoo is alive, displaying the latest innovations in web design like reduced fonts, minimalistic headers, upgraded eCommerce tools, improved navigation, and lesser whitespace.

Tokoo is also optimized for speed and search engines and compatible with mobile devices and web browsers.

Sky’s the limit, so make sure you utilize Tokoo to its full potential.

More info / Download Demo

12. Gloss

You can easily dive into the affiliate marketing space with Gloss. It is an easy-to-use WordPress theme that is perfect for news and review websites, blogs and even online magazines.

The topic and niche are unimportant, as Gloss alters to any out-of-the-box.

And you do not even need prior experience due to the codeless web development process. Pre-defined samples are also at your disposal once you unbox the Gloss kit.

Gloss is robust and practical, ideal for novice and expert users.

Other goodness of Gloss includes Elementor builder, Slider Revolution, Contact Form 7, MailChimp, store section, 3 header styles and more.

More info / Download Demo

13. Nutrico

If you are passionate about health, nutrition, fitness and wellness, you better check Nutrico. It is a versatile and user-friendly WordPress theme that supports affiliate marketing.

And if you would like to go against the norm, you can also employ Nutrico for hammering out landing pages, which you can use in your paid marketing campaigns.

The options are many when you gain access to Nutrico.

The bundle will discover 5 headers, 6 homes, extensive documentation, a drag-and-drop page builder, Slider Revolution, and 30+ custom-made elements.

Easily alter the default web design of Nutrico to fit your requirements and start your online project quickly.

More info / Download Demo

Which is the best WordPress theme for affiliate marketing?

MagPlus is the best affiliate marketing WordPress theme. 4,000 marketers use it worldwide to create countless websites and generate millions in revenue. It works well for Top 10 style listicle websites and single product review sites.

Another honorable mention is Gillion theme by Shufflehound. Its clean design and versatility work great for any affiliate website.
