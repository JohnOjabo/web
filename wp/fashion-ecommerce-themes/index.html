22 Best Fashion Ecommerce Themes for WordPress 2023

  • May 10, 2023
  • Aigars Silkalns
  • Theme Collections
  • 1 Comment

These are WordPress’s trendiest and easiest-to-use fashion eCommerce themes to kick-start your online business.

With our review, nothing holds you back from creating the perfect online fashion store.

It’s SIMPLE.

Pick the theme that resonates with you best confidently – that’s it!

Yes, we did the necessary testing to ensure all the options below are high quality and trustworthy.

Plus, they are all compatible with WooCommerce and drag-n-drop method – a winning combo for beginners.

Launch a fashion webshop with style – within minutes.

Best WooCommerce Fashion WordPress Themes

1. Jevelin

Nowadays, starting an online fashion store is not complicated.

Thanks to Jevelin, a fashion eCommerce theme, you can start making moves with just a click. A fully dedicated demo is available just for the fashion and apparel industry.

The clean and minimal design ensures it shines on your must-have goods. While Jevelin works well out of the box, you can always step things up and enrich them with your branding regulations.

Coding and other advanced tasks are unnecessary when working with Jevelin. Integrating the WPBakery page builder allows every user to get the most out of Jevelin comfortably.

Experience great success with Jevelin.

More info / Download Demo

2. April

The April fashion eCommerce theme is nothing short of a beauty. It features over 14 beautifully designed homepage layouts you can import all with just a click.

This theme is pre-bundled with premium plugins like Element Template and Slider Revolution. Plus, it brings you 10 custom widgets and 8 header-style templates.

Moreover, the April fashion eCommerce theme is fully compatible with WooCommerce, Contact Form 7, WPML, and other popular plugins.

It costs $60 and comes with 6 months of support (for free).

More info / Download Demo

3. Bazaar

Bazaar is a modern, minimalistic eCommerce theme with a gorgeous layout. It features multiple shop listing layouts such as masonry, carousel, and animated layouts.

The Bazaar eCommerce theme comes with customizable shortcodes to list product pairs in animated or carousel format, display product info, and more.

You can supercharge your portfolio pages, selectively listing products, including grouped products, external, and downloadable ones.

This theme lets you choose pre-loader icons, different header types, and cool background effects such as video and parallax.

Every aspect of the Bazaar theme is customizable without the need to do the code work.

More info / Download Demo

4. Amely

Amely is an elegant WordPress eCommerce theme with 23+ beautifully designed homepage layouts.

If you plan a promotional campaign surrounding just one product, Amely is the way to go.

Moreover, Amely features 25+ shortcodes, 3+ blog page layouts, 4+ pre-made header templates, and other cool pages such as the 404 not found and coming soon.

From a purely WooCommerce standpoint, the Amely theme brings in a lot of unique features such as bundle buying, wishlist, compare, and Instagram shop.

These features help boost your sales or, at the least, make people add them to your shopping cart! A little additional marketing, and they’re on your side.

More info / Download Demo

5. Halena

Halena is a clean and minimalistic WordPress WooCommerce theme for online fashion shops.

Each layout is carefully designed to work smoothly on desktop and mobile devices.

This theme is fully AJAX powered, making tasks such as previewing products, filtering product listings, searching and adding to the cart – effortless.

Halena features 4 single-page layouts and has a unique hotspot pin in each product description. This creates a frictionless experience for customers browsing individual products.

Halena is a beautiful WordPress WooCommerce theme that helps you build the perfect online store with multiple header styles, powerful theme functions and typography options.

More info / Download Demo

6. Adorn

Adorn is a beautifully designed and clean WordPress fashion eCommerce theme.

You can add customizable product listing, pricing tables, team members, and portfolios in multiple modes.

Adron also has separate logo and header styles for dark and light layouts, parallax image sections and more.

Moreover, it features social widgets for Instagram and Twitter, has 5 font icon packs, and integrated search. The Adorn theme packs powerful features such as the quick view, drop-down cart widget, popup subscription form, and user login widget.

If you’d like to launch a minimalist online store, go to Adorn.

More info / Download Demo

7. Sway

Build a beautiful, high-performing fashion eCommerce website with Sway. If you want to create an online store that does not look too traditional, you now know where to start.

Sway is all you need, as it covers front and internal pages, blocks, elements, and all the other necessary material an eCommerce website requires.

The theme’s structure is also 100% fluid and cross-browser compatible, so performance will always be first-class. In addition, you can brand Sway to your needs, start a blog, and use testimonials to build customer trust.

More info / Download Demo

8. Shoperific

Shoperific is here to serve your ideas and realize them in a snap of a finger. If you are looking for a fashion eCommerce theme, Shoperific is the right option.

Not only does Shoperific come with multiple ready-made demos designed and established for the fashion industry, but you can also modify the available contents at free will.

Other goodies of Shoperific come in the form of unique product display, wishlist, item compare, child theme, retina readiness, and mobile-friendliness.

This theme is compatible with WooCommerce, Contact Form 7, WPML, drag-and-drop page editor, and numerous handy elements and components.

Starting on the web, creating your fashion store is effortless with Shoperific.

More info / Download Demo

9. Hongo

Hongo is a multi-concept solution that works exceptionally well as a fashion eCommerce theme. With the bundle of ready-made demos, you will find the right web design for your intention.

On top of that, Hongo also supports drag and drop page building. It gives you a chance to modify the default appearance without programming.

Moreover, Hongo includes various collection styles, different product pages, product quick view, a ton of pixel-perfect elements, and 100+ customizable inner pages.

Pre-defined headers, color swatches, interactive banners, product wishlist and compare, video support, and size guide are other specialties of the amazing Hongo.

More info / Download Demo

10. Zyra

Zyra is a modern WordPress eCommerce theme with 15+ beautifully designed homepage layouts, unlimited shop variations, and unique product and inner pages.

You can choose from 6 header layouts, 4 blog post layouts, 11 portfolio layouts, and 5 testimonial styles. In addition, you can customize the theme using parallax background sections, Google Maps integration, and more.

Zyra comes pre-bundled with WPBakery and Slider Revolution, supports RTL languages, and gives you access to the one-click demo data import!

Additionally, the theme has 4 custom post layouts, including testimonial, portfolio, team, and custom blocks. While these features are not a must for an eCommerce fashion store, it still helps you find that unique edge and stand apart from the competition.

More info / Download Demo

11. Moren

Moren is an original fashion eCommerce theme that will help distinguish your online business from the masses. You can select from many beautiful and trendy demos that enable you to start working on your website with a click.

From then on, you can enter your creative touch, transform the default settings to your liking and call it a day. Moren is straightforward, follows all the freshest regulations, and uses only modern technologies.

Your online fashion store will function great on all devices, ensuring a top-notch online shopping experience.

More info / Download Demo

12. Woncep

With 10+ stunning home demos and multiple inner layouts, Woncep is a neat fashion eCommerce WordPress theme to launch an online store quickly. It rocks a gorgeous, minimal look to distraction-free display all your apparel items.

Some more amenities of Woncep are fast loading speed, animations, social media, mega menu, blog, and parallax effect. The layouts, sections, and elements are also entirely configurable, so you can quickly fine-tune Woncep to your desire.

Also, if you need any additional help, flip through the documentation or contact the friendly support team.

More info / Download Demo

13. Shopkeeper

Shopkeeper is one of the best-selling fashion eCommerce WordPress themes on the market. This theme includes bold typography, minimalist design, and a light and dark theme. It also offers a flexible shop layout, with an off-canvas sidebar or an always on-screen sidebar.

Choose from 3 different scrolling options, including infinite scroll, classic pagination, and the good ol Load More button.

You can also pick from over 4 product page listings, 3 blog post layouts, and unlimited header styles. With Shopkeeper, you can create the fashion store of your dreams. Go!

More info / Download Demo

14. Voevod

Voevod is a stylish WordPress WooCommerce theme with 12+ homepage layouts that support one-click installation.

Moreover, the Voevod theme enables you to customize your stores product page with multiple effects. These include the carousel, animation, and classic grid layouts.

It comes pre-bundled with the most popular plugins at no additional charge, unlocking the extraordinary potential you deserve. Voevod theme is fully compatible with Contact Form 7 and the WPML plugin.

You’re on the right path with Voevod!

More info / Download Demo

15. Elessi

Elessi is one of my favorite online fashion store themes for WordPress. Being fully powered by AJAX, this theme comes with 35+ beautiful homepage layouts and custom filters for shop and category.

You can customize the shop layout by placing the sidebar on the top, left, right, or no sidebar!

Elessi also has the product single sticky feature. It has a customizable gallery with a thumbnail and slider.

The theme doesn’t shy away from the off-canvas cart, wishlist, product comparison, RTL language support, and a live search. You get it all!

More info / Download Demo

16. Flatsome

Flatsome is one of the most popular fashion eCommerce themes on ThemeForest. It comes with 300+ pre-defined layouts and sections ready to mix and match.

Flatsome uses a custom page builder, which hosts a library of ready-to-use elements. This theme allows you to customize every page of your fashion eCommerce store.

Flatsome theme is one of the best in the market, scoring a 98% in Google Page Speed.

Flatsome is a pretty cool theme in an entrepreneurs arsenal with additional features such as smart lazy loading, live search, and automatic theme updates!

More info / Download Demo

17. Fey

If creativity and originality are your cups of tea, Fey is the fashion eCommerce theme to consider. It is a tool that will easily set you apart from the competition.

To stand out, you need to make some noise with Fey. Thanks to all the ready-made materials and features, you can start online with Fey in just a few clicks.

Pick the demo, introduce your branding regulations, and you are ready to rock and roll.

That’s how simple it is!

Once you pick the demo, import it with a click and go from there. You can use the sample precisely as is, or you can customize it further. You’ll create a banging product with Fey.

More info / Download Demo

18. Grosso

Grosso is a responsive, mobile-ready, and browser-compatible fashion eCommerce theme.

To push jewelry, you can create a classic apparel store, an online sunglass shop, or even an eCommerce page. But you can go way beyond that with Grosso.

There is everything you need to be packed in one bundle of features. Shoppers can navigate your page using the super convenient mega menu and create personal wishlists.

Moreover, Grosso sports off-canvas AJAX search features and sidebar, custom login forms, reviews and supports deals and promotions.

You also get free lifetime updates, so your online fashion shop keeps looking fresh and modern forever.

More info / Download Demo

19. Zoli

Zoli is a fashion eCommerce theme with a minimal, trendy, and modern design. The layout of this dope WordPress theme is fluid to instantly adapt to any device for a smooth operation.

Also, Zoli is perfectly in tune with web browsers and optimized for fast loading speed and SEO.

Each layout of Zoli is unique, ready to push your products and get the sales going strong.

Along with front pages, Zoli includes all the necessary internal sections, product and shop pages, lookbook, categories, and more.

Elementor page builder, Slider Revolution, Contact Form 7, and WooCommerce are just some of the extensions of Zoli that make it a complete eCommerce tool.

More info / Download Demo

20. Aurum

Aurum has a special welcome landing page that lets users select their country. It then redirects them to the regional store. Apart from this unique feature, Aurum is beautifully designed – retina display ready and optimized for mobile.

You can customize multiple aspects of the theme with the WPBakery page builder, Layer Slider, and mega menu plugins.

With WooCommerce specific features such as wishlist and infinite scroll, Aurum is a great selection for your global fashion eCommerce store.

More info / Download Demo

21. Oxygen

Oxygen is another one of my favorite fashion eCommerce themes on this list. It creates a very pleasant experience, making each product stand out beautifully!

Apart from 7 unique theme designs, the Oxygen theme brings you many customizable features. These include shopping cart ribbons, quick previews, product carousels, a side menu, and a wishlist.

You can offer your online store in any language of your choice with WPML compatibility.

But if you’d like to take things to the next level, Oxygen’s child theme support makes things happen. You’re in total control because you deserve it.

More info / Download Demo

22. Sober

Sober is a premium, all-in-one WooCommerce theme with dedicated layouts for clean, minimal, classic, and modern Bootstrap-based stores.

The developers made Sober AJAX-powered, with excellent features such as live search and quick product view. This theme is translation-ready with support for the WPML plugin.

Sober has 16+ homepage layouts, 6 header headers, 3 shops, and 5 individual product pages.

Best part?

You can import the pre-defined layouts with a single click! Plus, every aspect of the theme is customizable to enrich it with your branding and other creative touches.
