1. Divi

If you are a writer and you want to create a professional writing website, look no further than Divi.

It gives users the tools to craft a page worthy of hosting well-written content. You don’t need to possess a computer programming degree to use Divi.

It is one of the most accessible and user-friendly products on WordPress.

Currently, your website is available in 32 languages, and there is RTL (right to left) writing support for languages such as Japanese or Hebrew.

From the start, users will gain access to 100+ pre-designed website layouts.

But Divi Builder enables the manipulation of website blocks, making it easier to organize content.

The layouts are responsive, and this theme has multiple typography settings.

What to makes a great WordPress theme for writers & authors?

  • Responsive and cross-browser compatible: With any of the themes below, you should not be concerned about the flexibility of your website. They work flawlessly on all popular web browsers and devices – and that’s what’s expected from any up-to-the-minute theme.
  • 100% creative freedom: Even though many of you will want to work with a theme’s sample material, it’s important to note that you can modify and fine-tune the default settings, too. You can create magical things for your text-heavy website with a page builder.
  • Little to no distractions: When sharing your stories and other compelling content, it is important to keep the reading experience distraction-free. Focus on as little special effects and animations as possible, as you do not want the reader to leave early.
  • SEO-friendly: As a long-term project, your website should be search engine optimized. Thanks to these WordPress themes for writers, they take care of it for you out of the box. Of course, you can always expand its possibilities by introducing a dedicated SEO plugin.
  • Loading speed: Today, especially with the high mobile use, your website needs to load at the speed of lightning – not be otherwise. And this is something light and optimized WordPress themes secure you with.
  • About page: As a writer, do not avoid sharing your story with the world via a well-thought-out about page. You can make this with a WordPress theme since executing it requires little work. Write the text, add an image, and you are done.
  • eCommerce section: Very likely, you have or will have books and eBooks to sell. With this in mind, have a section on your website to offer your masterpieces to your loyal fans. Thanks to the themes below, they either come with an integrated function or are easily expandable.
  • Testimonials/reviews: Nowadays, it is pretty easy to build customer trust. You can do it with honest testimonials and reviews, which you can include on your website and sales pages. Let readers’ opinions help market your books, which can be highly rewarding.
