14 Best Web Hosting For A Personal Website 2023

  • July 17, 2023
  • Rok Krivec
  • Hosting
  • 22 Comments

If you would like to host your website, these best web hosting services for a personal website are your go-to choices. Nowadays, more and more people are building websites for personal and business reasons. But even a website made only for personal use can mysteriously become popular, and you later turn it into a money-making machine. Always plan long-term when putting together a page for your online project.

Food bloggers, travel enthusiasts, fashionistas, gamers, photographers, designers, you name it, a personal website is a fantastic way to share your knowledge and expertise with the world. Or maybe you are launching a special event that you want to share with your close ones or impress the employer with an online resume. Bear in mind, that the latter gives you a higher chance of landing the job you always wanted.

However, for many, the idea of putting together and hosting a website is almost unimaginable—no need to feel pressure when it comes to this task. As you will quickly discover, the best web hosting providers for a personal website we gathered are very user-friendly. Your idea will become a reality in a concise space of time. And there is no need to be outsourcing a specific operation. You can do it all on your own. If you need help, each host comes ready with a friendly support team.

Now let’s look at the best hosting services for personal websites and get your project rocking.

Best Personal Website Hosting Services

1. Bluehost

Best overall for small personal websites.

WordPress is as big as it is. You probably use it for whatever online project you are running or plan to launch. Bluehost is one of your best web hosting providers if that is the case. However, you do not have to limit yourself in any shape or form. You can use whatever other CMS and still rule the online world with a fantastic web hosting service for a personal website like Bluehost. Do what feels right for you and your website and Bluehost will take care of all the rest.

With over 2 million websites using Bluehost, you know you are safe with the service. They offer you everything you need plus a whole bunch of other features. And whenever your website takes off and grows beyond expectation, you can instantly upgrade to a larger plan and keep your website running continuously. FYI, before you even begin, you would need a domain name. If you still do not have one, Bluehost offers you one for free.

If you still need to decide what would fit best for you, Bluehost’s support is ready to take care of you and guide you toward successfully launching your website. Moreover, with their help, you will get the most out of the package you will pick. It would be advisable to go with the cheapest option, more than enough for small and new pages.

Bluehost pricing:

The cheapest option is $2.95/month for the first year, which will renew at $10.99/month for the second year. However, you can maintain the lower price by subscribing to a long-term plan.

Monthly billing: There is no option for monthly billing with Bluehost.

Get Hosting

2. Hostinger

Cheapest hosting that still rocks solid.

Here we bring you a cheap web hosting service for all your websites, whether a blog, a portfolio or an online resume. No matter what website you bring into being, Hostinger will handle them all. What’s best, once you grow your page to a certain extent, Hostinger will grow with you easily. Meaning, you never have to worry about your page not being live because it always will be.

At only $1.99 a month, Hostinger offers a complete solution for hosting one website. Bear in mind, there are also two other plans available, but starting, the Single Web Hosting package will be more than ideal for you. It offers you 30 GB SSD disk space and 100 GB bandwidth, weekly backups and a 99% uptime guarantee. No need to question yourself where to find the best and the cheapest web host for your page, go with Hostinger.

Hostinger pricing:

Cheapest option: $1.99/month for a single website. $2.99/month to host up to 100 websites and get a free domain name. These are prices when you pay for an entire year upfront.

Monthly billing starts at $11.99/per month, and I would strongly recommend going for a long-term plan which will help you save up to 80%. If you need to test your website first, you can go with the 30-day trial to see if Hostinger is right for you, but never use monthly billing because it will be costly.

Get Hosting

3. SiteGround

Fastest shared hosting for personal websites.

The grand web hosting for a personal website provider, SiteGround, will impress every new page owner. Ideal for sites for creatives and even small businesses. SiteGround offers three shared hosting plans: StartUp, GrowBig, and GoGeek. These are not the cheapest on the market; however, they aim to provide you with premium features even for the entry-level package.

Starting low, you will get a solid hosting solution for your single page with StartUp. With 10 GB of web space and up to 10,000 monthly visits, your freshly crafted website or a small existing page get all the crucial hosting features. Your data is stored on SSD drivers for the fastest loading speeds. Free site transfer and setup, free daily backups, and even free SSLs are all that comes with the StartUp.

Offering the most fabulous website experience will be a breeze with SiteGround. It is the hosting company that focuses on the essentials first. Their plans might not have many features compared to some of the competition. But that is not necessarily a bad thing. As a matter of fact, with fewer goodies to think about, they can focus on the actual performance. It is all about the must-haves for SiteGround’s web hosting services for your pages.

You get even more information and a personal view of their services by reading our SiteGround review.

Siteground pricing:

Cheapest option: The first year starts at $2.99/month when paid for the entire year upfront with a free domain name included.

Monthly billing is available, starting at $19.99/mo and you get a domain name for free. It renews at $19.99/year.

Get Hosting

4. DreamHost

Your page, which you always dreamt about launching, gets realized with DreamHost easily. It is superb web hosting for a personal website with cutting-edge technology. Depending on your needs, there are two hostings that you can go with, WordPress or shared. Both are fully loaded with excellent features for your website to shine online. Since DreamHost uses SSD servers, your website’s performance will be way faster than HDD servers. They automatically scale up bandwidth when needed to ensure your website will always be live.

You can host unlimited websites and domain names within one account regarding shared hosting’s features. Pre-installed WordPress connects you with the CMS straight away. Furthermore, personal email accounts are sorted and secured with a free SSL certificate. If you want your website search engine optimized, SSL is essential.

The simplicity allows everyone to use DreamHost and manage personal websites effectively. One of DreamHost’s payable features is a page builder called Remixer to turn yourself into a website building wizard. Once you start using it, you will not believe how easy it is to develop the exact website you fancy.

Let’s move things without further ado and build and host a fresh page for a personal project with DreamHost.

5. Ionos

If you are an individual professional, a creative, or a job seeker who plans to attract employers with an online CV, hosting a personal website comes down to basic needs. If you do not plan to invest a whole bunch into paid marketing to see instant results, the fundamentals are more than enough. Here’s Ionos Hosting, which has a special deal ready to launch your website.

With Ionos Basic package, you get 100 GB storage, 1 free domain for the first 12 months, and an SSL certificate. For scalability, level 1 is included, which means, up to 2.5 GB RAM, half is always guaranteed. This allows your website to work uninterrupted. Scalability goes up to level 5 in just one click. Be always prepared.

Ionos has a range of amazing features ready for your dynamic personal websites. With gzip, the amount of data web server transfers can be reduced, which you can see as faster loading times. In correlation, less data usage calls for a better-performing website, especially for mobile users. That is where speed matters most.

If you do not have a website yet, take Ionos site builder to your advantage and construct the ideal online presence.

By choosing Ionos to host a personal page, you ensure the best user experience and the safest.

HostGator has great shared plans ready for every individual and exclusive WordPress packages. By being one of the leading hosting companies in the world, HostGator gives you a lot for your buck. Their basic shared plan is a flexible web hosting solution for a personal website, Picking the cheapest option. Like every other plan, you get a free website builder, zero-cost page transfer, and a $200 marketing budget. You can use the latter with Google and Bing for a quick boost.

With over 8 million domains under their wing, HostGator is ideal for a personal website. Every step you make towards a successful launch of your page, you always have HostGator’s support at your service. It will be especially handy if you are new to the business and set up your first page. Even if you have little or zero prior experience, prepare to surprise yourself with how much you can accomplish independently. Meanwhile, they will transfer both for you if you already have a website and a domain name. Having it all in one place to operate and manage is very advantageous. If you might be scrolling up and down this collection and are not 100% sure which hosting company fits you perfectly, HostGator might be the way to go. It is an all-in-one solution for a prosperous web appearance.

Crafting a personalized page for your project has never been easier, and you can do it all from one account—no need to jump from one location to another. Any help you may need, a team of friendly staff is yours 24/7.

InMotion is the optimal web hosting service to host your beautiful websites and help you achieve great success. All of your pages are optimized for speed, secure, and always up-to-date with automatic updates.

11. Cloudways

Cloudways is a web hosting service focused on one thing only: cloud web hosting. They offer fully managed cloud hosting, of which all plans have one fantastic feature: pay as you go. In other words, you only pay for the resources you need and nothing else. This makes it very user-friendly for everyone to benefit from. Being the type of a hosting provider focusing only on the managed cloud hosting platform, makes them one of the best at what they do. If you are looking for something else, Cloudways is not the host to go with.

What is fantastic about Cloudways is that it’s very flexible and scalable services. You pick the desired infrastructure, like Amazon, Google, or DigitalOcean, and start from there. Depending on which you decide to go with, the location of the servers may vary. More than 25 global data centers ensure Cloudways have servers the closest to your audience. Without a second thought, these guys give you a full solution for your website. Their cloud-based hosting is an excellent alternative to a VPS.

Like any other from our list of the best web hosting for personal website providers, Cloudways has pro-level customer services. Adjust and modify the managed cloud hosting they are experts at to your precise needs. To make it all even more tempting, Cloudways offers you a free trial to test it out and will migrate your current website for free.

Updated: We have recently started using it extensively for many projects. For example, all our website templates are now hosted on Cloudways. Previously we used VPS, and while it was a more cost-efficient solution, we found that it took too much time to keep up with all the security updates and maintenance. We are now big Cloudways fans.
