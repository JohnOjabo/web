How to Choose the Right WordPress Hosting Theme?

1. Choose the design wisely

You do not need to invest too much thought into this one. After all, when using any of the themes from the list here, they promise a well-thought-look that will wow all your visitors and potential customers/clients. In many cases, you will even want to stick to the default appearance, as it already fits your style perfectly.

2. Customization

You can do that if you want to take things to the next level, modifying the theme to your liking. But let the idea of performing customizations not intimidate you. Luckily, visual editing is available to every theme via the simplistic method of dragging and dropping. The theme uses a popular builder, like Elementor, WPBakery or King Composer, or they even have their custom-made one.

To summarize, you can tweak the WordPress theme to the last detail.

3. User experience

It is important to offer a great but excellent user experience with your web hosting website. Even though there is a very good chance that the majority of your users will be using a desktop device to browse your website, it’s still necessary to make it fully compatible with smartphones and tablets.

They may look around the web for a host on a mobile device, but they will later seal the deal on a desktop.

Moreover, you need to take care of fast loading, too. Hey, if a potential client lands on a website of a host and domain registrar and it takes ages to load – will they trust them? Of course not.

With a predefined theme, you need not worry about any of the above. They are optimized to ensure an outstanding website performance that you and your users deserve.

4. Site navigability

Even though you may be starting slow, at some point, you will surely have many goodies to offer your customers. With that in mind, you must ensure that all the different services and other whatnot are quick to access, even from a mobile device.

You would want to have the main offerings and pricing on the first page to get their hands on the hosting that suits them right from the get-go. But great categorization and menu should also not be omitted. Again, make sure the user experience is always on your mind.

5. WHMCS integration

Many hosting WordPress themes also come with convenient WHMCS integration and WHMCS Bridge. This further simplifies your web hosting and domain registrar business operation. WHMCS, “Web Host Manager Complete Solution,” tells you more than enough.

All of these premium themes come with outstanding quality and pixel-perfect design. If you find that some amazing themes are missing, please leave a comment below, and I will list it here.

Of course, if you have some extra questions before purchasing, please leave your questions below. I have used several themes and know what you can and can’t do with them.
