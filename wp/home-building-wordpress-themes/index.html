Home Building WordPress Themes for Construction and Renovation Websites

  • June 25, 2023
  • Julia Starlark
  • Theme Collections
  • 0 Comments

Home building companies need a responsive and stylish website to attract and retain customers. The main goal is to showcase designs and past projects to convince people to choose their services. Two important features of a home builder’s website theme are media support for pictures and videos and an interface for client inquiries and consultations.

Updating the website regularly with new content will increase visitor numbers and local market recognition, leading to more sales. Since most home builders lack coding knowledge, they require a user-friendly pre-made website setup. Choosing the right theme is crucial for website success. Here are 12 top WordPress themes for home builders to simplify the process.

Dwell – Home Building & Renovation WordPress Theme

Dwell is a modern and contemporary Home Design WordPress Theme that is perfectly suited for various businesses related to home repair, interior design, house renovation, and remodeling. It offers a range of features and functionalities that cater to the specific needs of these industries. The theme includes 7 pre-designed homepage demos tailored for home-building and construction websites. These demos provide a starting point for creating a unique and visually appealing website for your business. With the inclusion of Slider Revolution, you can access a powerful slider tool that can showcase your work and captivate your audience.

Dwell integrates with the popular Elementor page builder, allowing you to easily customize and create your page layouts according to your preferences and requirements. This flexibility gives you full control over the design of your website, ensuring it reflects your brand and showcases your services effectively. Furthermore, Dwell is compatible with various plugins, such as WPML for multilingual support, Smash Balloon Instagram Feed for displaying Instagram content, Image Hotspot by DevVN for interactive image presentations, and elegro Crypto Payment for accepting cryptocurrency payments.

Dom – House Services Elementor WordPress Theme

Dom WordPress theme offers a variety of layouts that encompass everything you need to showcase your business to potential clients. Dom features online appointment booking and management, allowing customers to easily schedule and manage their appointments through your website. Advanced contact forms are also included, enabling seamless communication between your company and clients. The theme is fully compatible with the popular WooCommerce plugin, allowing you to set up an online store and sell your services or products directly from your website.

With premade modules for services, prices, and galleries, Dom provides convenient options for presenting your offerings to your target audience. The theme is highly customizable through advanced options, ensuring you can tailor Dom to fit your specific branding and design preferences. It is also compatible with various plugins such as LatePoint for appointment scheduling, Elementor Page Builder for easy page layout customization, Contact Form 7 for managing contact forms, and elegro Crypto Payment for accepting cryptocurrency payments.

Hampton – Home Design and Renovation WordPress Theme

Hampton is a modern and contemporary Home Design WordPress Theme that is perfectly suited for businesses in the home repair, interior design, house renovation, and remodeling industries. With Hampton, you gain access to a range of features and functionalities designed to enhance your website and business. The theme offers unique homepage layouts that provide visitors with a visually appealing and engaging experience. The inclusion of Slider Revolution as the home slider allows you to create stunning and dynamic slide presentations.

To assist in building custom page designs, Hampton is compatible with WPBakery. This integration enables you to easily create and customize the layout and content of your website. The theme is also compatible with several premium plugins that enhance its functionality. WPML ensures multilingual support, allowing you to reach a wider audience. Essential Grid provides versatile grid layouts for showcasing your portfolio or services. Booked Appointments helps you manage and schedule appointments with clients. Instagram Feed allows you to integrate your Instagram account and display your latest photos. These plugins, along with others, contribute to the overall success of your company’s website.

Corbesier – Architecture & Design Interior & Exterior Modern WordPress Theme

Corbesier is a responsive and clean WordPress theme well-suited for modern interior design agencies, exterior design bureaus, architecture firms, construction bureaus, building companies, or individual designers. It offers a fresh and contemporary design that will effectively showcase your services and skills. The theme is built with the Elementor page builder, which provides a user-friendly interface for creating and customizing your website’s layout and content. This allows you to have full control over the design and appearance of your site. Corbesier is optimized to work seamlessly with popular plugins such as Slider Revolution, Mailchimp for WordPress, elegro Crypto Payment, and Contact Form 7.

These plugins enhance the functionality of your website and provide additional features such as dynamic sliders, newsletter integration, cryptocurrency payment options, and customizable contact forms. Moreover, Corbesier is fully compatible with WooCommerce, allowing you to set up an online store and sell your interior design products or services directly from your website. This integration enables you to manage your products, track orders, and provide a seamless shopping experience for your customers.

Built with the Elementor page builder, W&D provides an easy-to-use platform for creating and customizing your website’s design and content. You have full control over the layout and appearance of your site, allowing you to create a unique and tailored online presence. m2 – Construction Equipments and Building Tools Store WordPress Theme

m2 is a trendy and colorful WordPress theme for equipment and tools stores, construction equipment companies, building parts firms, and tools agencies. With three beautiful homepage layouts to choose from, you can create a visually appealing and engaging website that captures the attention of your visitors. The theme also provides dozens of easy-to-use shortcodes, allowing you to add various elements and functionalities to your pages with just a few clicks. m2 includes ready-made pages for showcasing your projects, portfolio, services, products, contacts, team, and blog.

The theme is fully compatible with WooCommerce, the leading e-commerce plugin for WordPress. This means you can set up a complete online store, manage your inventory, process orders, and accept payments seamlessly.
