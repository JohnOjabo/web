36 Best Minimalist WordPress Themes 2023

  • March 15, 2023
  • Aigars
  • Theme Collections
  • 29 Comments

Collection of the best WordPress minimalist themes for the blog, portfolio, corporate, and other awesome minimal-style websites.

The main focus of these minimal and simple themes is your content instead of design elements. However, these tools look stunning even without heavy effects and design elements.

Minimalist design has been around forever, but it has become trendy due to the success of Material Design and Flat Design, which have inspired designers. Minimalistic design is here to stay, and more and more websites follow these trends.

Why WordPress? It is the widely used blog and CMS platform, powering 43% of the entire web. (Makes. Sense.)

Let’s enjoy and use these minimalist themes together!

Best Fast & Minimalist WordPress Themes

1. Divi

Divi is a mobile-friendly and minimal WordPress multipurpose theme.

It’s a decidedly elegant and deeply appealing WordPress theme, constructed as a generalized all-purpose tool to create minimalist yet powerful websites.

Divi suits any niche in an unfalteringly stylish and clean presentation and keeps your website focus-oriented.

Divi Builder allows for a completely visual, coding-free block-based page crafting dynamic that is easily mastered and yields high-quality results within minutes.

Experience the joy of a clean, minimal website with Divi today!

2. Kalium

If you are searching for a minimal WordPress theme that can highlight your portfolio or blog, check out Kalium. Kalium is multilingual ready, thanks to WPML plugin support.

This theme has something for everyone, starting with distinct templates.

Slider Revolution is incorporated, resulting in more slider flexibility for the user. You may edit your animations or transitions, making something truly yours.

Make wonderful galleries, streamlined slide shows, fantastic content slideshows, and remarkable effects.

Be it a simple style, or something that looks fancy, that’s up to you – Kalium is ready for all your ideas.

3. Jevelin

Jevelin is a colorful and minimal WordPress multipurpose theme. You can build endless styles of websites with Jevelin and customize them to your heart’s content.

The tool is also ready for deployment as a blogging platform with several different blog layouts to express yourself however you want.

You get the best platform for easy and quick minimal website building with Jevelin.

4. Soledad

Soledad is a beautiful and minimal WordPress blog and magazine website theme. It’s a powerful and flexible tool with the utmost care and meticulous attention to detail.

This theme results from a clean design philosophy melding with multi-conceptuality that lets Soledad easily and quickly adapt to any magazine, news purpose, field, industry or concept.

You can do it all with Soledad like a champ.

5. Gillion (Trending Item)

Gillion is an incredibly easy-to-use and minimal WordPress blog theme. No distracting details and unnecessary effects distract visitors from the main thing – your content.

Gillion is the perfect theme for showcasing your articles with a quick setup and easy customization.

It is SEO and translation friendly to get more visitors to your website.

You also get help from Gillion’s professionals if you need extra support building your new page.

6. Folie

Folie is a 100% live editing WordPress theme, the best of its kind. It is thus intuitive with a minimal responsive layout and is easy to use. It uses WPBakery Page Builder and needs no coding skills!

Folie was designed for both inexperienced and advanced users with modes for each.

Folie uses Page2page communication to copy and paste elements easily. It is the first theme that saves previous changes with a click!

It has premium plugins integration like MailChimp (forms) and Slider Revolution (galleries).

Folie also has WooCommerce integration for shops and WPML compatibility for translations. And you also get free updates and support.

It supports lots of heavy image, video and animation formats. Arnold gives special importance to fonts, using Google Fonts and Awesome Fonts Icon.

It has one-click demo access and a multilanguage right-to-left plugin. You will get 6 months of support from BWSM.

Create gorgeous settings, structures and designs with Arnold quickly. But first, taste and witness its beauty by accessing the live demo preview.

The layout is optimized for speed and SEO, too. The performance will always be first-class, impressing everyone visiting your page, with Webify.
