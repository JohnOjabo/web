Best Simple WordPress Themes For Beginners

1. Kalium

Kalium is a powerful, easy-to-use WordPress multipurpose portfolio and online shop website theme.

Built around the premium WPBakery page builder and endowed by the smooth Slider Revolution, achieving your website’s professional look and finish is easy with Kalium (without coding experience).

A ton of elements to do beautiful things can be dragged and dropped into the fray directly from the WPBakery, while incredibly theme options put you in complete control of your Kalium!

Tons of helpful demo websites for various purposes are included for an instant start.

2. Jevelin

Jevelin is a beautiful and highly functional WordPress multipurpose theme. It has a flexible collection of attractive demo websites and purpose page templates for all general and niche applications.

With Jevelin, customizing your website is an easy, drag and drop process empowered by an impressive page builder. Thanks to professional support, you will never be at a loss in making the most of Jevelin’s advanced features.

At the same time, regular updates continuously improve Jevelin’s coding and add more content and templates for you to work with. Jevelin is ready to make a difference!

3. Gillion (Trending Item)

If you want an affordable WordPress theme for beginners, Gillion is for you! The design of this theme is elegant and minimalistic.

Gillion uses the famous Bootstrap Framework for extreme flexibility. The documentation is solid and support even better.

The layout of your website can be changed, and it does not require any special knowledge. You can simply drag and drop the section, arranging them at will. Gillion is translation-ready, compatible with the WPML plugin.

All demo data is included, allowing you to modify and build upon it, so you don’t have to start from scratch.

4. Infinite

Infinite is a high quality multipurpose WordPress theme. It features 30+ demos on various topics with a one-click demo import each.

It handles secondary and floating navigation. Have fun reorganizing sidebars, color skins, headers, and footers.

You can create thumbnails on your personal blogs, portfolio pages, and galleries. Infinite uses Lightbox, Slider Revolution and Layerider.

CSS3 animations and parallax are also featured to spice things up. Infinite is WooCommerce and WPML integrated.

You get constant updates and customer support for free! Play around, have fun, and build your site with the best!

Soledad is a powerfully WordPress multi-concept blog and magazine website theme.

It’s a potent theme decked out with an ambitious and expansive set of over 250 Home Demo pages, 250 Live Customizer options, and over 1,000 slider and blog combinations possible.

Soledad is a truly marvelously simplified theme, with a streamlined, polished trim on every element.

This theme is an endlessly shape-shifting theme that, regardless of your specific blog or magazine website niche application or field of interest.

Keep it simple with Soledad!

The best part?
