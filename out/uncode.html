Uncode is a pixel-perfect creative WordPress Theme for any kind of website (portfolio, agency, freelance, blog) and a top WooCommerce Theme for shops (eCommerce, online store, business). Uncode is designed with incredible attention to detail, flexibility, and performance. In addition, the Uncode Website Builder includes responsive templates and allows you to edit your pages without touching a single line of code: anything you can think of can be built!

Uncode is clean, modern, and suitable for every niche: agency, freelance, designer, web designer, developer, architect, portfolio, photography, business, services, marketing, startup, corporate, event, wedding, artist, music, restaurant, blog, magazine, shop and eCommerce.

Import and modify over 70 professionally designed demo layouts. Additionally, with the Uncode Wireframes Plugin, you can import also 550+ section templates that can be combined to create a complete website or independent landing pages.

Uncode Creative & WooCommerce WordPress Theme

Uncode is one of the best-selling creative themes of all time with more than 100.000+ growing sales on ThemeForest.If you are searching for an innovative theme, Uncode is a top recommendation. Lets find out why:

Frontend Page Builder
Uncode is packed with an enhanced version of the famous WPBakery Page Builder. Use the classic Backend Editor, or the new Frontend Editor, which allows you to edit your pages on the frontend of your website: a fantastic experience that allows for a much faster design. Choose the one you like best!

Demos Library
With Uncodes ever-growing library of 70+ customizable demos, you can jumpstart your website development in a few clicks by importing the pre-built layouts with our 1-Click Importer, and start customizing it right away.

One-Click Import
Easily import Demo Contents with a single mouse click and start tweaking in no time. The Demo Contents Import provides you with a technical replica of the homepages, posts, portfolios, sliders, and product layouts you see in our main demo.

Wireframes Plugin
Wireframes is the new free plugin bundled with Uncode. Wireframes allows you to easily import over 550 carefully designed section templates that can be easily combined to create a complete website or independent landing pages.

Pixel-Perfect
The difference between something good and something great is attention to detail. Uncode Theme is ultra professional, smooth, and sleek, with a clean, modern layout, for almost any possible and impossible designs.

World-Class Design
Uncode is designed with pixel-perfect attention to all details. Great design can do a lot to help improve your marketing efforts, and it does more than just help your content stand out and look good. Make it simple but significant!

Impressive Showcase
What we promise you are not words, so we invite you to see the results. If you want to have a piece of evidence visit the Showcase with great examples of brand-new websites created by our customers with the Uncode Creative Theme.

Most Popular
Since its debut, Uncode has been an incredible success among designers and agencies who used it to create their websites. In addition to this, Uncode was appreciated by the most influential web design blogs and magazines, including Designmodo, Speckyboy Magazine, Hongkiat, Forbes, Web Designer Ledger, InspiredMag, Line25, and more.

Content Block
The Content Block is an Uncode feature not found in any other theme. The Content Block allows users to create custom sections of content, and apply them in different parts of the website: headers, pages, footers, etc. With the Automatic Query option, you can use the Content Block also to create a design matrix to style your archive, category pages, and Related Posts.

Dynamic Srctset Images
The Dynamic Srcset creates responsive cropped images and serves only the appropriate version depending on the visitors viewport and device based on the srcset attribute. The Dynamic Srcset considers the devices resolution, updates the srcset value, and then serves the right-sized image for each situation.

Hierarchical Theme Options
Take full control of your layouts with the hierarchical options system that leaves nothing behind. Just imagine and create with Uncode. Theme Options are the backbone of Uncode. They give you full control over your website and allow you to customize the design without resorting to a single line of code.

Advanced Grid System
Grid is the core of any professional layout. Uncode is based on a superior Grid System. This unique approach pushes layout variation to the cutting edge with full-width and limit row, vertical alignments, gaps control, vertical rhythm settings, equal-heights, off-grid shifts, responsive, animations, and much more.

Integrated Header Builder
Forget about theme presets and the need to rely on additional plugins. With Uncode, you can visually design your headers and sliders directly with the Page Builder. Put whatever modules you want in your header and spice it up with all the options provided by the Page Builder. Moreover, its enhanced by dynamic content options.

Unique Posts and Media Gallery
These unique Uncodes modules create all the variations of blog posts, portfolios, products, and image galleries that you see on our demos. There are over 100+ options you can combine. Masonry, Metro, Justify or Carousel style, thumbnail size, and ratio, items filtering, pagination and gap, reordering options, and responsive settings, just to name a few.

Light and Dark Skins
When using Uncode you will see frequent references to Light and Dark skins. Skins are settings that allow you to switch your text and elements color automatically following the background coloration to grant optimal contrast. Make great design much easier!

Colors Palette
Save any color as a Palette color with the click of a button. Then choose the global color from the Palette to use it with any element. When you change the color, that change will take effect everywhere the color is used.

Privacy Plugin (GDPR)
With the Privacy Plugin, bundled with Uncode, its possible to block usage of third-party services up to the explicit consent of the user from the Privacy Preferences in compliance with the GDPR. With the unique Consent Logic, you can include and exclude content based on the users consent.

WooCommerce Product Builder
Thanks the WooCommerce Product Builder its possible to create Single Product layouts with the Page Builder, and to use these layouts as a matrix for all or specific products. Visually and without coding. Hook your clients better than the competition!

Custom WooCommerce Cart, Checkout, My Account and Account Forms
With unique custom Cart, Checkout, My Account, Login/Register, and Empty Cart pages that reflect your brand values, you can create the ultimate shopping experience consistent with your design and your clients needs.

WooCommerce extensive options
Design pixel-perfect eCommerce websites with in-depth integration with WooCommerce, visually and without coding, thanks to the new Posts module special WooCommerce features, the Quick-View, the Wishlist, the Widgetised Filters, the Catalog Mode, and much more

Dynamic Elements and options
With the Dynamic Elements, you can create custom template and designs for your content with personalized layouts populated by data from various sources, and use it across your website. Are you ready to free your imagination?

Slides Scroll
Slides Scroll provides a toolset of options you can use to transform any one-page design into an exciting presentation. With Slides Scroll, users can smoothly move between sections of the page, by simply scrolling their mouse or pressing the arrow keys. Plus its enhanced with awesome transitions.

Powerful One-Page
One-page or Scrolling websites are being adopted by some of the worlds largest brands. These websites provide users with a fast, clean, and straightforward scroll navigation that is both attractive and effective. With Uncode you can create one-page scrolling websites with just a few clicks.

Shape Dividers
Using the Shape Dividers feature, you can add attractive and unique separators to your web pages. There are 30+ default shapes to pick from, or you can create your own by uploading an SVG code. No matter which shape you choose, you can fully customize the look of your divider by flipping, inverting, resizing, and coloring it. This feature helps you create dynamic transitions that break up your content in a visually stunning way.

Animated Headings
With the advanced Animated Headings and Animated Highlighting features, you can bring your pages to life and grab visitors attention quickly. The new animations are an excellent way to engage users and encourage them to explore the rest of your content.

WooCommerce Integration
With perfect design integration, Uncode is compatible, with the most popular WooCommerce. Get incredible flexibility and amazing functionalities for your shop design, plus the Advanced Drag & Drop Product Builder with performant configurable Ajax product Filters, and modern and effective Variation Swatches.

Menu layouts
Uncode comes packed with ten different menu layouts. Any menu has advanced options and effects: top right and left, justify, center, center split, and double, overlay and overlay center, off-canvas, and lateral. There are also effects such as automatic color change, sticky mode, hide and show, and shrink.

Extensive Typography Options
Uncode seamlessly integrates fonts from Google Fonts, Adobe Fonts, FontSquirrel, and Fontdeck. Want to use a custom font? No problem! All font options are controlled in the theme options panel. Set different font sizes, families, margins, and letter spacing.

Powered Media Library
Our enhanced version of the WordPress Media Library can swallow any media embed you are adding to your WordPress. This is a great advantage in managing the content you can control.

Album Galleries
Uncode introduces a new workflow for media gallery creation and management. The Album Galleries Album Galleries are completely independent media galleries located within another parent gallery. With this feature, its possible to open an entire Media Gallery from just one thumbnail and create impressive one-page portfolios.

100% Responsive
Uncode is a Responsive Theme with pixel perfect accuracy: it automatically detects the screen size and adjust the content accordingly to provide fully responsive and optimized sites. Switch to mobile view, and tweak layouts to make it look perfect on any device.

Enhanced Backgrounds
With Uncode, its easy to add background images, gradient colors, overlay, Parallax effect, Ken Burns and Zoom animations, spectacular blend mode effects and videos to make your background come alive.

Creative blog, portfolio and products layouts
Customize each and every page easily through global theme options or individual page option overrides, that allow you to modify that only relate to the current or selected page. Just choose your style and start to create.

Coming Soon and Maintenance Mode
Create a custom Coming Soon or Maintenance Mode page with the Page Builder and use this page while you are working on your site in private mode, while visitors see a the Coming Soon or the Maintenance Mode page.

Page Builder Elements
All modules are developed in-house: Row, Columns, Posts, Media Gallery, Single Media, Heading, Text Block, Icon Box, Button, Content Block VC, Content Slider, Pricing Table, List, Counter, Countdown, Share, Google Maps, Before and After, Author Profile, Info Box, Social Icons, Copyright, Contact Form 7, Message Box, Tabs, Accordions, and much more.

Multilanguage certified and RTL support
Uncode Theme has been certified by the official WPML team. WPML is the most popular multilanguage solution for WordPress. With WPML, you can translate pages, posts, custom types, taxonomy, menus, and even the themes texts. Additionally, Uncode offers full support for RTL (right to left) languages.

Regular updates
Uncode releases continued value packed feature updates based on user-requested features and demands. Continued codebase improvements for performance enhancements and future maintenance. Plus every update is FREE to anyone who has bought a license.

Power Elite Support
Uncode is developed and supported by a professional Power Elite Team that has sold more than $5 million dollars worth of items on Envato. We take care of our customers, and thats why we offer you world-class support. You also have at your disposal comprehensive online documentation that is continuously updated.
